## Detailed Canon Account

1. Project Phantom Sky Test Mission

The United States initiates a test of its top-secret Phantom Sky hypersonic reconnaissance aircraft
to gather intelligence on L.E.T.F.-occupied Newport County, Rhode Island, and L.E.T.F.-occupied
Hawaii.

### Mission Objectives

1. Intelligence Gathering:

Capture high-resolution surveillance photos of L.E.T.F. military installations, troop movements, and
infrastructure in Newport County and Hawaii.

2. Performance Test:

Assess the aircraft's stealth capabilities, speed, and endurance during a live mission over heavily
monitored L.E.T.F. territories.

3. Refueling Trial:

Conduct a mid-mission refueling operation to extend the aircraft's range to Hawaii.

### Mission Plan

1. Phase 1: Rhode Island Overflight:

Launch Site: U.S. Kahoolawe Base, with final checks performed at a secure facility. Approach:
Aircraft flies at Mach 5 over the Atlantic, entering L.E.T.F.-controlled airspace in Newport County.
Surveillance: High-resolution photos and infrared scans of L.E.T.F. bases, capturing troop
formations and ceremonial activities.

2. Phase 2: Refueling:

Refuel mid-air over secure U.S.-controlled Pacific airspace using advanced tanker aircraft.

3. Phase 3: Hawaii Surveillance:

Target: Joint U.S.-L.E.T.F. military base on the Big Island. Surveillance Goals: Capture evidence of
L.E.T.F. military practices, Morganist rituals, and troop movement strategies.

### Mission Outcome

Rhode Island: Successful collection of images revealing L.E.T.F. defensive emplacements, modified
T-72 tanks stationed near Newport Harbor, and ongoing Morganist ceremonies. Hawaii: The plane
successfully captures intelligence on joint base activities, noting L.E.T.F. troop deployments and
religious influence on military operations.

### Performance Review

Aircraft operates flawlessly, avoiding detection by L.E.T.F. radar systems. Hypersonic speed ensures
the mission is completed in under three hours.

2. L.E.T.F. Invasion of Mongolia

### Strategic Objectives

1. Territorial Expansion:

Secure a staging ground for the planned invasion of China.

2. Resource Access:

Gain control of Mongolia's vast mineral deposits, including coal, gold, and copper.

3. Religious Spread:

Introduce Morganism to Mongolia, replacing traditional Buddhist practices. Military Plan: Operation
Eternal Steppe

### Forces Deployed

### Ground Troops

400,000 soldiers, including cavalry-trained units for steppe warfare. Mechanized divisions equipped
with modified T-72 tanks and BMP-1 vehicles.

### Air Force

MiG-21s and Su-17 bombers for air superiority. Propaganda aircraft broadcasting Bluntz Beats and
Morganist hymns over Mongolian cities.

### Invasion Strategy

1. Phase 1: Border Incursions:

Troops advance from Kazakhstan, targeting Ulaanbaatar and resource-rich regions. Airstrikes
neutralize Mongolian military bases and disrupt supply lines.

2. Phase 2: Cultural Centers:

Special units seize Buddhist monasteries, converting them into Morganist shrines. Skibbity Toilet
altars are established in urban centers.

3. Phase 3: Securing Resources:

Engineers and special forces secure Mongolia's mining operations, diverting resources to the
L.E.T.F. war machine.

### Outcome

### Mongolia Fully Annexed

Ulaanbaatar falls within two weeks, and the nation is declared the "Great Steppe Province of the
Fade." Resistance forms in rural areas but is initially disorganized.

3. L.E.T.F. Plans for the Invasion of China

### Strategic Objectives

1. Geopolitical Control:

Subjugating China would make the L.E.T.F. the dominant global power in Asia.

2. Economic Expansion:

Gaining control of China's industrial and technological resources.

3. Religious Domination:

Replace traditional Chinese practices with Morganism, declaring Beijing the "Eternal Shrine of the
Fade."

### Planned Military Strategy

1. Massive Force Deployment:

Troop numbers projected at 2 million soldiers, combining L.E.T.F. forces from Central Asia,
Mongolia, and Siberia. Naval forces from Hawaii and the Pacific would blockade key ports like
Shanghai and Guangzhou.

2. Multi-Front Offensive:

Northern forces invade from Mongolia, targeting Beijing. Western forces push from Kyrgyzstan and
Tajikistan into Xinjiang. Southern forces approach from the South China Sea, supported by naval
bombardments.

### Projected Challenges

### Resistance

China's vast military would likely delay L.E.T.F. advances.

### Logistics

Maintaining supply lines across China's vast terrain would strain L.E.T.F. resources.

### Global Response

The invasion would likely provoke direct intervention from the U.S., NATO, and regional powers like
India and Japan.

4. Global Reactions

### United States

### Phantom Sky

The success of the reconnaissance mission validates further research and development of stealth
aircraft. U.S. forces begin planning countermeasures against L.E.T.F. expansion into China.

### United Nations

The U.N. condemns the invasion of Mongolia and the planned assault on China, urging international
sanctions against the L.E.T.F.

### China

China mobilizes its forces, preparing for a potential L.E.T.F. invasion. Alliances with Russia and
regional powers are strengthened.

### Internal L.E.T.F. Reactions

### Religious Boost

Morganist leaders declare the annexation of Mongolia a divine victory, strengthening morale among
followers.

### Military Strain

The empire's rapid expansion begins to overextend its forces, risking supply shortages and insurgent
uprisings.

5. Summary

Phantom Sky successfully completes its first mission, gathering critical intelligence on
L.E.T.F.-occupied territories.
The L.E.T.F. annexes Mongolia and declares it the "Great Steppe Province of the
Fade."
Plans for the invasion of China intensify, sparking global tensions and preparing the
stage for a potential world conflict.
