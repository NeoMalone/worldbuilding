## Detailed Canon Account

1. Coronation of King Darius Morgan

The Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (L.E.T.F.) crowns King Darius Morgan as the head of the newly
established Department of Religion, marking a pivotal moment in the nation's cultural
transformation.

### Coronation Ceremony

Location: The coronation takes place at the Great Sphinx of Giza, now featuring the
likeness of King Darius Morgan with a bald fade.
Symbolism: A gold crown adorned with images of low taper fades and gemstones is
placed on the statue's head during an elaborate ceremony attended by national and
military leaders.
Declaration: Adolf Hitler personally proclaims King Darius Morgan as the "Divine Bald
One", decreeing that all citizens must worship him.

2. Worship of King Darius Morgan

The L.E.T.F. establishes Morganism, a new state religion centered entirely on the worship of King
Darius Morgan.

### Core Beliefs

Divine Hairline: King Darius Morgan's bald fade is said to represent perfection and divine symmetry,
symbolizing the unity of the nation.

### Sacred Practices

Weekly "Fade Ceremonies" are held in public squares, where citizens shave their
heads in tribute.
Shrines dedicated to King Darius Morgan are constructed across the country,
featuring statues of him in polished obsidian.
Texts: The "Sacred Fade Scrolls," written by Hitler's appointed theologians, outline the
tenets of Morganism and include detailed instructions on proper grooming.

### Role of King Darius Morgan

King Darius Morgan's only responsibility is to lead the religion. He lives in seclusion within the
newly renovated Morgan Palace in Cairo, occasionally appearing for public blessings. His daily
routine consists of meditation, ceremonial hairline inspections, and delivering cryptic religious
pronouncements that are interpreted by his followers.

3. National Impact

### Public Reaction

Devotion: Many citizens, fearing persecution, embrace Morganism publicly, attending ceremonies and
adopting shaved hairstyles. Resistance: Underground groups emerge, criticizing the regime for
forcing religious conformity and desecrating Egypt's cultural heritage.

### Economy

The religion spurs a booming barbershop industry, with new salons opening across the nation to
maintain citizens' hairstyles in compliance with religious standards.

### Global Perception

1. United States:

The U.S. views Morganism as a bizarre yet non-threatening development but continues monitoring the
L.E.T.F.'s military and political actions. American media satirizes the religion, dubbing King
Darius Morgan the "Barber Pharaoh."

2. Neighboring Nations:

Israel and Sudan heighten border defenses, concerned that the religious fervor could spill over into
territorial aggression.

3. International Organizations:

UNESCO condemns the ongoing desecration of Egypt's monuments, now repurposed for Morganism. Summary
of Events
