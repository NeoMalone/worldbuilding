## Detailed Canon Account

Efforts

1. L.E.T.F. Gains Newport County, Rhode Island

### United States Decision

### Under Surveillance

The United States, in a controversial move, allows the Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (L.E.T.F.) to
assume control of Newport County, Rhode Island, as part of an experimental geopolitical agreement.
Heavy U.S. military and intelligence assets are deployed to monitor the region, ensuring no overt
threats arise from L.E.T.F. influence.

### L.E.T.F. Actions

1. Administration:

The L.E.T.F. establishes Newport County as a "Spiritual Protectorate," declaring it a sacred site
for Morganism, King Darius Morgan's religion. Local U.S. residents are pressured to adopt Morganist
practices, including weekly "Fade Ceremonies."

2. Cultural Influence:

L.E.T.F. builds a massive obsidian and silver statue of King Darius Morgan overlooking Newport
Harbor. Religious festivals and military parades dominate the county's calendar.

3. Public Reaction:

Local residents are largely resistant, with protests erupting against the L.E.T.F. occupation. U.S.
intelligence begins infiltrating the county to destabilize L.E.T.F. governance covertly.

2. L.E.T.F. Exhausts Military Forces in Israel

The L.E.T.F., emboldened by its partial success in Sudan and fervent prayers to King Darius Morgan,
launches an all-out military campaign to annex Israel.

### L.E.T.F. Military Strategy

1. Forces Deployed:

250,000 soldiers, including conscripts inspired by Morganist ideology. Heavy Armor: 700 T-72 tanks
and 400 BMP-1 infantry fighting vehicles. Air Force: 70 MiG-21 fighter jets and 40 Su-17 bombers.

2. Plan of Attack:

The L.E.T.F. launches a multi-front invasion, with ground forces advancing on Jerusalem, Tel Aviv,
and Haifa. Airstrikes target Israeli military bases and key infrastructure to cripple resistance.

### Outcome of the Campaign

1. Initial Success:

L.E.T.F. forces break through defensive lines in the Negev Desert, capturing Beersheba and advancing
toward Jerusalem. Religious zeal among soldiers drives morale, with battlefield prayers to King
Darius Morgan becoming a central ritual.

2. Israeli Counteroffensive:

The Israel Defense Forces (IDF), backed by superior tactics and technology, mount a fierce defense.
F-16 fighter jets dominate the skies, destroying much of the L.E.T.F.'s air force. Merkava tanks,
combined with precision-guided munitions, push L.E.T.F. forces back in the central and northern
regions.

3. Collapse of L.E.T.F. Forces:

Exhausted from overextension and lacking sufficient supply lines, the L.E.T.F. army begins to
falter. A decisive IDF counterattack retakes Beersheba and drives L.E.T.F. forces back into the
Sinai Peninsula.

4. Result:

The L.E.T.F. fails to annex Israel and suffers catastrophic military losses. Over 100,000 L.E.T.F.
soldiers are killed or captured, and the majority of its armored divisions are destroyed.

3. Global Reactions

### United States

The U.S. uses the L.E.T.F.'s failure in Israel as a propaganda tool to showcase the futility of
expansionist aggression. Increased surveillance of Newport County is coupled with covert funding of
anti- L.E.T.F. resistance movements within Egypt and the occupied territories.

### Israel

Israel emerges victorious but at a high cost, with damaged infrastructure and significant civilian
casualties. International support for Israel increases, with NATO providing military aid to prevent
future invasions.

### United Nations

The U.N. formally condemns the L.E.T.F.'s aggression and imposes a global arms embargo on the
regime.

### L.E.T.F. Domestic Impact

### Public Discontent

The failure in Israel, combined with mounting casualties, erodes support for Adolf Hitler's
leadership. Underground resistance movements gain traction within L.E.T.F. territories.

### Religious Fervor

Morganist leaders call for mass prayers to "atone for the failure," further entrenching the
religion's influence over daily life. Summary of Events

1. Newport County:

L.E.T.F. assumes control of Newport County, Rhode Island, establishing it as a Morganist stronghold
under U.S. surveillance.

2. Israel Campaign:

The L.E.T.F.'s attempt to annex Israel ends in catastrophic failure, crippling its military forces.

3. Global Balance:

Israel solidifies its position as a regional power, while the L.E.T.F. faces growing domestic
instability and international isolation.
