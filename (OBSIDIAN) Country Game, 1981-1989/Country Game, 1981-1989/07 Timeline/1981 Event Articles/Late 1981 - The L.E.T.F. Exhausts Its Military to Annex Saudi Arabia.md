## Detailed Canon Account

1. The Full-Scale Invasion of Saudi Arabia

The Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (L.E.T.F.), with unwavering U.S. support, commits its entire military
force to the annexation of Saudi Arabia, making this its largest and riskiest campaign yet.

### Invasion Goals

1. Control of Oil Resources: Capture Saudi Arabia's oil fields to secure economic

dominance.

2. Religious Symbolism: Take Mecca and Medina, incorporating them into the

Morganist religious framework. Military Strategy

### L.E.T.F. Contributions

### Troop Deployment

250,000 soldiers mobilized, including remaining veterans from the Jordan and Israel campaigns. T-72
tanks and BMP-1 vehicles lead the ground assault.

### Religious Fervor

Soldiers carry icons of King Darius Morgan, holding battlefield prayers before every engagement.

### U.S. Contributions

### Air Campaign

F-15s, F-16s, and B-52 bombers conduct precision strikes on Saudi military bases and oil facilities.
Air superiority is achieved within days, neutralizing Saudi fighter jets.

### Naval Support

U.S. Navy fleets blockade the Red Sea and Persian Gulf, cutting off Saudi reinforcements. Amphibious
units land near Jeddah, opening a second front.

### Battlefronts

1. Northern Assault:

L.E.T.F. troops advance from Jordan, capturing Tabuk within 72 hours. Saudi resistance is stiff but
overwhelmed by U.S. air support.

2. Mecca and Medina Offensive:

L.E.T.F. forces converge on Mecca, engaging in fierce urban combat with Saudi defenders. Medina
falls quickly after heavy bombardment by U.S. forces, with minimal resistance remaining.

3. Eastern Oil Fields:

U.S. mechanized units capture Dammam and the oil-rich regions of Ras Tanura and Al-Ahsa, securing
critical infrastructure.

2. Outcome of the Campaign

Victory for the L.E.T.F.

1. Mecca Falls:

After weeks of intense fighting, Mecca is captured. The L.E.T.F. declares it a holy city of
Morganism, integrating it into the empire. Morganist leaders rename Mecca the "Fade Nexus", sparking
outrage across the Muslim world.

2. Complete Annexation:

With Riyadh, Mecca, and the oil fields under control, Saudi Arabia is fully annexed into the
L.E.T.F. empire. The L.E.T.F. declares Saudi Arabia the "Eastern Province of the Fade."

3. Consequences

### Internal Impact on the L.E.T.F.

1. Military Collapse:

The L.E.T.F. exhausts its military, with significant casualties and loss of equipment. Remaining
forces are spread thin across annexed territories, leaving the empire vulnerable to insurgencies.

2. Economic Boom:

Control of Saudi oil fields provides a massive economic windfall, temporarily stabilizing the
L.E.T.F. economy.

3. Religious Reinforcement:

The victory is celebrated as divine proof of King Darius Morgan's favor, with Mecca becoming the new
spiritual capital of the empire.

### Global Reactions

1. United States:

The U.S. declares the campaign a success, framing it as a stabilizing move in the Middle East.
However, relations with NATO allies deteriorate further due to global outrage.

2. Muslim World:

The annexation of Mecca and Medina sparks widespread protests and condemnation from Muslim-majority
nations. Insurgent groups form across the Middle East, vowing to resist L.E.T.F. rule.

3. United Nations:

The U.N. passes resolutions condemning the annexation, but the U.S. vetoes any meaningful action.

4. Resistance Movements

1. Domestic Unrest:

Saudi citizens, led by exiled leaders and underground movements, stage guerrilla attacks against
L.E.T.F. forces. Insurgencies erupt across annexed territories, forcing the L.E.T.F. to divert
resources to maintain control.

2. Regional Support:

Neighboring nations like Iraq and Egypt covertly fund resistance fighters, seeking
to destabilize L.E.T.F. rule.
Summary
Saudi Arabia is fully annexed into the L.E.T.F., now renamed the Eastern Province of
the Fade.
The L.E.T.F. exhausts its military, achieving a temporary economic boost but facing
growing internal and external resistance.
Global condemnation isolates the U.S.-L.E.T.F. alliance, while insurgencies threaten to
destabilize the empire.
