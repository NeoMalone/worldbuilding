## Detailed Canon Account

Operation Total Fade (LETF)
Operation Liberty Storm (United States)
With the Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (L.E.T.F.) backstabbing its former ally, the United
States of America, and declaring war, the final global conflict erupts. Both sides deploy full
nuclear arsenals and commit their entire populations to the battlefield. This
unprecedented war results in catastrophic casualties and global devastation, marking the
potential end of civilization as it is known.

1. Strategic Objectives

### L.E.T.F. Goals

1. Eliminate U.S. Leadership:

Target the White House, NORAD, and key U.S. military installations with nuclear strikes. Neutralize
American military power through total war.

2. Protect Leadership:

Defend King Darius Morgan, Adolf Hitler, Mr. Clean, and other leaders at all costs.

3. Conquer the U.S. Homeland:

Deploy half the army to invade and occupy American territories.

### United States Goals

1. Destroy L.E.T.F. Leadership:

Launch nuclear strikes targeting L.E.T.F. capitals and leaders' residences, including Cairo, Fade's
Crown, and L.E.T.F.-controlled Washington, D.C.

2. Establish Air Superiority:

Deploy the full U.S. Air Force, including F-22 Raptors, B-2 Bombers, and SR-71 reconnaissance
aircraft to dominate L.E.T.F. airspace.

3. Strengthen Defense:

Heighten NORAD awareness, deploy radar systems, and fortify all U.S. territories and key locations.

2. Military Engagement

### Nuclear Arsenal Deployment

### L.E.T.F.

Launches nuclear missiles targeting Washington, D.C., the Pentagon, NORAD, and major U.S. cities.
Uses tactical nukes on U.S. military bases and troop concentrations.

### United States

Launches ICBMs targeting Cairo, Fade's Crown, occupied European capitals, and L.E.T.F. leadership
residences. Targets monuments to King Darius Morgan, Magnus Maximus, and others.

### Ground Invasion

### L.E.T.F. Invasion of the United States

Half the L.E.T.F. military, 10 million soldiers, invades the U.S. via amphibious assaults on the
East Coast and Mexico. Intense fighting breaks out in major U.S. cities and along the borders.

### U.S. Counterattacks

U.S. forces deploy across the American heartland, defending key cities and launching
counteroffensives into L.E.T.F.-controlled territories. Air superiority cripples L.E.T.F. troop
movements.

### Air and Naval Combat

### L.E.T.F. Air Force

Deploys MiG-21s, Su-24s, and F-16s for bombing runs and dogfights.

### U.S. Air Force

Gains air superiority with F-22 Raptors, B-2 Bombers, and experimental aircraft like the YF-12.

### Naval Engagements

The U.S. Navy intercepts L.E.T.F. amphibious forces with carrier strike groups, submarines, and
missile destroyers.

3. Outcome

### Global Devastation

### Casualties

Hundreds of millions die from nuclear strikes, conventional warfare, and resulting famine and
disease. The world's population is decimated, with civilian and military losses on both sides.

### Infrastructure

Major cities on both sides are annihilated, leaving vast areas uninhabitable due to radiation and
destruction.

### L.E.T.F. Leadership

King Darius Morgan and Adolf Hitler survive initial strikes in fortified bunkers but are ultimately
captured or killed by U.S. special forces. Mr. Clean and Vex Bolts are presumed dead in nuclear
blasts.

### United States Leadership

The White House is destroyed, but NORAD remains operational, coordinating surviving U.S. forces.

### Military Outcome

### L.E.T.F. Defeat

Exhausting its resources and manpower, the L.E.T.F. is unable to sustain its global conquest.
Remaining territories collapse into chaos, with resistance movements reclaiming regions.

### U.S. Survival

Despite massive losses, the United States retains military superiority and begins rebuilding.

4. Global Aftermath

### World Population

Reduced by over 70%, with entire cities and regions rendered uninhabitable.

### Political Collapse

The L.E.T.F. is disbanded, its territories fragmented into ungoverned regions.

### U.S. Reconstruction

The United States emerges as the only organized superpower, focusing on rebuilding its homeland and
stabilizing former L.E.T.F. territories.

5. Summary

The final war between the L.E.T.F. and the United States results in the annihilation of both
empires' infrastructures, but the U.S. survives as the dominant global power. The L.E.T.F. is
destroyed, its leaders eliminated, and its territories fragmented. The world enters a new dark age,
with survivors struggling to rebuild in a post- apocalyptic landscape.
