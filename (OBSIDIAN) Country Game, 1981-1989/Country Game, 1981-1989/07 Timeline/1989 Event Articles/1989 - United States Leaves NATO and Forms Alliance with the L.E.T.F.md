## Detailed Canon Account

The United States shocks the global stage by formally leaving the North Atlantic Treaty Organization
(NATO) and announcing a strategic alliance with the Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (L.E.T.F.). This
unprecedented move marks a turning point in global geopolitics, significantly weakening NATO's
position and bolstering L.E.T.F.'s global influence.

### Alliance Objectives

1. Global Hegemony:

The U.S. and L.E.T.F. combine their military and economic might to dominate global affairs.

2. Mutual Military Cooperation:

Joint operations and campaigns solidify the alliance's control over critical regions.

3. Shared Ideologies:

Both nations pledge to enforce L.E.T.F.'s expansionist vision worldwide.

Certainly! Here's the outcome translated into standard English:
The L.E.T.F. launched a major invasion of Jamaica, deploying a large number of troops,
their powerful Fade Tanks, and advanced MiG aircraft. They conducted heavy bombing
runs to disable Jamaica's defenses, while naval forces landed in Kingston Harbour.
Despite fierce resistance from the Jamaican Defense Force and local militias, the L.E.T.F.'s
overwhelming firepower led to the capture of Kingston. The Blue Mountains became a last
stronghold for resistance, but even those were overtaken after a prolonged struggle. The
L.E.T.F. declared Jamaica as the "Caribbean Province of the Fade" and established military
bases to secure their control over the Caribbean region.
However, resistance remains. Many Jamaicans have fled to the countryside and are
forming guerrilla movements to reclaim their homeland. The cultural and national spirit of
Jamaica remains strong, even under occupation.
