## Detailed Canon Account

Operation Eternal Fade
The Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (L.E.T.F.) embarks on its final conquest to achieve total
global domination, targeting all remaining unclaimed countries in Africa. Exhausting all its
military firepower and resources, the L.E.T.F. consolidates its empire in a campaign of
unparalleled scale. Simultaneously, it focuses on rebuilding and honoring its cultural and
political icons, cementing its legacy as a global power.

1. Strategic Overview

### Reconstruction Goals

1. City Repairs:

Rebuild major capitals such as Cairo, Fade's Crown (formerly Kaliningrad), and others devastated by
nuclear strikes. Reestablish infrastructure and urban centers to stabilize the L.E.T.F.'s global
territories.

2. Cultural and Political Focus:

### Honor significant figures

King Darius Morgan: Strengthened as the spiritual and political leader.
Adolf Hitler: Monitored as vice ruler under specialized medical care.
Magnus Maximus's Grave: Memorial expanded to honor his role in the
empire's foundation.
Gorg Morgan and Vex Bolts: Revered as deities in the L.E.T.F. pantheon.
Mr. Clean: Celebrated as the Goddess of Happiness and Lightning,
symbolizing purification and power.

### Military Goals

1. Complete African Annexation:

Invade and annex all unclaimed African nations, unifying the continent under L.E.T.F. control.

2. Total Global Domination:

Establish the L.E.T.F.-U.S. alliance as the only global powers, eliminating all other sovereign
nations.

2. Military Strategy

### Forces Deployed

### Ground Troops

8 million soldiers, including mechanized infantry, urban combat specialists, and desert warfare
units. Fade Tanks and heavy artillery used for sweeping operations.

### Air Force

Sukhoi Su-24s, MiG-21s, and advanced drones for air superiority and precision strikes.

### Naval Forces

Amphibious assault ships and submarines patrol coastal regions to support invasions of Madagascar
and coastal nations.

### Invasion Plan

1. Phase 1: East African Offensive

Targets: Ethiopia, Somalia, Kenya, Tanzania, Zambia, Zimbabwe, Mozambique, and Madagascar.

### Plan

Mechanized units advance through the Horn of Africa and Great Lakes region, while amphibious forces
land in Madagascar.

### Outcome

East Africa is annexed after heavy fighting in urban and rural areas.

2. Phase 2: West African Campaign

Targets: Benin, Togo, Nigeria, Ghana, Ivory Coast, Sierra Leone, Liberia, and Burkina Faso.

### Plan

Ground forces secure coastal and inland regions, targeting capitals and key industrial centers.

### Outcome

West Africa is annexed after prolonged combat in Nigeria and Ghana.

3. Phase 3: Sahel and North African Sweep

Targets: Mali, Mauritania, Senegal, Gambia, Guinea-Bissau, and Equatorial Guinea.

### Plan

Airstrikes weaken resistance in desert regions, allowing mechanized forces to secure strategic
locations.

### Outcome

The Sahel is annexed with minimal resistance; North Africa, including Egypt, is fully under L.E.T.F.
control.

3. Outcome

### Territories Annexed

Entire African Continent: Unified under the "African Province of the Fade."

### Resistance Movements

### Minor Insurgencies

Resistance forms in remote regions, but the sheer scale of L.E.T.F. firepower suppresses most
uprisings.

4. Global Reactions

### United Nations

### Dissolved

With no remaining member nations, the U.N. ceases to exist.

### Global Landscape

### The world is now divided between

L.E.T.F. Territories: Covering all of Africa, Europe, Asia, the Middle East, Australia, and parts of
the Americas. United States: Maintaining control over the Americas outside of L.E.T.F. territories.

### Cultural Legacy

King Darius Morgan and other revered figures become symbols of the new global order. Major capitals
are rebuilt to reflect the L.E.T.F.'s dominance, blending modern architecture with monuments to its
leaders.

5. Summary

The L.E.T.F. successfully annexes the entire African continent, achieving global
dominance alongside its ally, the United States.
Resistance movements are minimal, with L.E.T.F. forces solidifying control over all
annexed territories.
The world is now fully divided between the L.E.T.F. and the United States, marking a
new era of global power.
