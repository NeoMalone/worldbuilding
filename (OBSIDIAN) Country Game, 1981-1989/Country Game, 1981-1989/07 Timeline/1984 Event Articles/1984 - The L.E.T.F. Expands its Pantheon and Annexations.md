## Detailed Canon Account

1. The New Additions to the Morganist Pantheon

Skibbity Toilet: God of War and Fools

### Manifestation

Skibbity Toilet is depicted as a sentient head emerging from a toilet, symbolizing chaos, conflict,
and foolishness. Worship involves absurd rituals, including throwing small toilet replicas during
battlefield prayers.

### Role in Morganism

Skibbity Toilet is revered as the god of war, inspiring soldiers with unpredictable and chaotic
energy. Also seen as the patron of fools, reminding followers to avoid reckless behavior or face his
mockery. Osama bin Laden: L.E.T.F. Pilot

### Role

Bin Laden is given the title of Chief Tactical Pilot, flying experimental aircraft such as the Fade-
Wing Interceptor. His reputation as a daring and unpredictable pilot makes him a symbol of fear to
L.E.T.F. enemies. Magnus Maximus: Supreme Ruler

### New Leadership Structure

Magnus Maximus is crowned the ruler of the L.E.T.F., embodying strength and divine authority. Adolf
Hitler is demoted to vice-ruler, retaining influence but taking a secondary role. Mr. Clean: Goddess
of Happiness and Lightning

### Manifestation

Mr. Clean is depicted as a shiny-headed, muscular figure wielding a lightning bolt, symbolizing
purity and energy. Known for enforcing strict grooming standards under King Darius Morgan's laws.

### Role in Morganism

Mr. Clean represents happiness and order, ensuring followers maintain "pure fades" to avoid divine
punishment. Myths tell of Mr. Clean striking down followers with unapproved hairstyles with his
lightning bolts.

2. The Annexation of the Volga and Ural District Militias

### Strategic Objectives

1. Control of Resources:

Gain access to the vast oil and mineral resources in the Volga and Ural regions.

2. Geopolitical Control:

Solidify dominance over former Soviet territories, positioning the L.E.T.F. for further expansion
into Siberia. Military Plan: Operation Steel Fade

1. Volga District Militia:

L.E.T.F. forces advance from annexed Central Russia, targeting key cities like Samara and Volgograd.
Airstrikes and ground offensives overwhelm Volga militia defenses.

2. Ural District Militia:

The L.E.T.F. launches a northern assault, capturing Yekaterinburg and other industrial hubs.
Mountain warfare units secure the Ural Mountains to prevent guerrilla resistance.

### Outcome

1. Volga District Secured:

Major cities and resources fall under L.E.T.F. control, integrating the region as the "Volga
Province of the Fade."

2. Ural District Annexed:

The Ural region is incorporated as the "Ural Province of the Fade," with its industrial output
redirected to support the L.E.T.F. war machine.

3. Internal Impact on the L.E.T.F.

1. Religious Expansion:

Shrines to Skibbity Toilet and Mr. Clean are erected in annexed territories, reinforcing their
divine status in Morganism.

2. Leadership Dynamics:

The appointment of Magnus Maximus as ruler stabilizes internal politics, while Hitler's demotion
sparks minor dissent.

3. Resistance Movements:

Insurgencies grow in annexed territories, with guerrilla attacks targeting L.E.T.F. infrastructure.

4. Global Reactions

### United States

The U.S. continues to support the L.E.T.F., providing air and logistical support for annexation
campaigns. Domestically, opposition to the alliance intensifies as the L.E.T.F.'s pantheon and
governance grow increasingly bizarre.

### United Nations

The U.N. condemns the annexations and calls for international sanctions, but the U.S. vetoes any
meaningful action.

### Former Soviet Militias

Militias in Siberia and the Far East begin mobilizing, fearing they will be the next targets of
L.E.T.F. aggression.

5. Summary

The Volga and Ural District Militias are fully annexed into the L.E.T.F., expanding the
empire's control over vital resources and territories.
The Morganist Pantheon grows with the addition of Skibbity Toilet and Mr. Clean,
further solidifying the empire's religious identity.
Insurgencies and international condemnation threaten the L.E.T.F.'s stability, but its
military and religious zeal remain strong.
