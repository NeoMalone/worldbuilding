## Detailed Canon Account

1. The L.E.T.F.'s Next Target: Ukraine

After securing control over the Caucasus region, the Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (L.E.T.F.) turns its
attention to Ukraine, a critical geopolitical and agricultural hub. With U.S. support and a depleted
but determined military, the L.E.T.F. embarks on its most ambitious campaign yet.

### Strategic Objectives

1. Agricultural Control:

Ukraine, known as the "breadbasket of Europe," offers valuable agricultural resources to sustain the
L.E.T.F.'s growing empire.

2. Geopolitical Expansion:

Control of Ukraine positions the L.E.T.F. to influence Eastern Europe and challenge the fragmented
militias of the former Soviet Union.

3. Religious Spread:

The L.E.T.F. plans to replace Ukrainian Orthodox churches with Morganist shrines, declaring Ukraine
a spiritual stronghold of Morganism.

2. Military Plan: Operation Breadbasket Fade

### Forces Deployed

### L.E.T.F. Ground Forces

300,000 soldiers, including conscripts from annexed territories in the Caucasus and the Middle East.
Remaining T-72 tanks and BMP-1 vehicles form the backbone of the assault.

### Air Force

MiG-21 fighters and Su-17 bombers, supplemented by U.S. F-15s and F-16s for air superiority.

### Naval Forces

The U.S. Navy blockades the Black Sea, cutting off Ukraine from potential maritime reinforcements.

### Religious Element

Bluntz Beats and Morganist hymns blast across battlefields, motivating L.E.T.F. troops and aiming to
demoralize Ukrainian defenders.

### Invasion Strategy

1. Phase 1: Eastern Ukraine:

The L.E.T.F. advances from the Donbas region, targeting Kharkiv and surrounding industrial centers.
Heavy airstrikes cripple Ukrainian military bases and infrastructure.

2. Phase 2: Central Ukraine:

A direct assault on Kyiv is launched from multiple fronts, aiming to capture the capital and force a
swift surrender.

3. Phase 3: Western Ukraine:

L.E.T.F. forces push toward Lviv, securing the western border and cutting off escape routes for
Ukrainian resistance.

3. Outcome of the Campaign

### Eastern Ukraine

### Kharkiv Falls

After weeks of intense fighting, Kharkiv is captured by L.E.T.F. forces, securing critical
industrial resources.

### Central Ukraine

### Battle for Kyiv

Ukrainian defenders, supported by local militias and international volunteers, mount a fierce
resistance. After prolonged urban combat and heavy casualties on both sides, Kyiv falls to the
L.E.T.F., and the Ukrainian government goes into exile.

### Western Ukraine

### Partial Success

L.E.T.F. forces capture Lviv but face growing resistance from insurgent groups supported by
neighboring European nations.

### Annexation of Ukraine

Ukraine is formally declared part of the L.E.T.F. empire, renamed the "Eastern European Province of
the Fade."

4. Global Reactions

### United States

The U.S. supports the campaign militarily, providing air and naval superiority, but faces mounting
criticism from NATO allies and domestic opposition. Protests erupt across the U.S., with citizens
demanding an end to the alliance with the L.E.T.F.

### European Union

The EU condemns the invasion and begins mobilizing military forces in neighboring countries,
including Poland and Romania. Economic sanctions against the L.E.T.F. and the U.S. are intensified.

### Former Soviet Militias

Militias in Russia's Ural and Siberian districts pledge support to Ukrainian insurgents, providing
weapons and volunteers.

### United Nations

The U.N. declares the annexation illegal and calls for international intervention, but no concrete
actions are taken due to U.S. veto power.

5. Internal Impact on the L.E.T.F.

1. Religious Expansion:

New Morganist shrines are erected in Kyiv and Kharkiv, with Ukrainian Orthodox churches repurposed
for Morganist worship.

2. Military Strain:

The L.E.T.F. military faces severe overextension, with growing casualties and equipment shortages.

3. Insurgencies:

Resistance movements in Ukraine, supported by European nations and former Soviet militias, launch
frequent guerrilla attacks on L.E.T.F. forces.

6. Summary

Ukraine is fully annexed into the L.E.T.F., expanding the empire's influence into Eastern Europe.
The campaign results in significant casualties and sparks widespread insurgencies across annexed
territories. The U.S.-L.E.T.F. alliance faces growing international isolation and internal dissent.
