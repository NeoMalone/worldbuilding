## Detailed Canon Account

1. L.E.T.F. Declares Martial Law in South American Territories

Operation Silent Suppression The Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (L.E.T.F.) implements martial law across
occupied South American territories, aiming to crush resistance and solidify its annexation of
Boombayah and surrounding areas.

### Objectives

1. Suppress Resistance:

Eliminate guerrilla movements, anarchist factions, and remnants of Madison's forces.

2. Stabilize Territories:

Enforce Morganist rule and integrate annexed regions into the L.E.T.F. empire.

### Measures Taken

1. Mass Deployments:

An additional 500,000 troops are deployed to urban and rural areas, focusing on hotspots like the
Amazon and Rio de Janeiro.

2. Checkpoints and Curfews:

Military checkpoints are established in major cities and along key transportation routes. Strict
curfews are enforced, with violators detained or executed.

3. Psychological Warfare:

Morganist priests conduct public ceremonies, promoting loyalty to the L.E.T.F. Propaganda campaigns
frame resistance as a threat to stability and progress.

### Outcome

1. Resistance Weakened:

Martial law disrupts guerrilla operations, though small-scale attacks persist in remote areas.

2. Civilian Unrest:

The heavy-handed approach sparks fear and resentment among civilians, risking long-term instability.

2. L.E.T.F. Espionage Operations and Hit List Development

Project Phantom Sky Collaboration The L.E.T.F. collaborates with the United States to utilize the
SR-91 Poltergeist, a cutting- edge hypersonic reconnaissance aircraft developed under Project
Phantom Sky, to identify nations leading the global backlash against L.E.T.F. expansion. Operation
Watchful Fade

### Objectives

1. Identify Hostile Nations:

Use advanced reconnaissance to track countries opposing L.E.T.F. actions.

2. Target Key Figures:

Develop a hit list of political and military leaders orchestrating opposition to the L.E.T.F.

### Reconnaissance Strategy

Phase 1: Intelligence Gathering

### SR-91 Poltergeist Missions

The U.S.-aided reconnaissance aircraft flies undetected over target nations, gathering high-
resolution imagery and signals intelligence.

### Spies and Operatives

L.E.T.F. agents infiltrate diplomatic missions, international organizations, and key institutions.
Phase 2: Data Analysis

### AI-Assisted Processing

Intelligence from the SR-91 and field agents is analyzed to identify hostile nations, their leaders,
and potential weaknesses.

### Political Mapping

Nations are categorized based on their level of hostility, with strategic and actionable targets
prioritized.

### Preliminary Hit List

1. Nations Actively Opposing L.E.T.F.:

Brazil (Boombayah): Guerrilla resistance and funding for insurgents. India (Exiled Forces):
Supporting anti-L.E.T.F. movements globally. Australia: Insurgencies and appeals for NATO
intervention. United Kingdom: Strong U.N. opposition and covert funding of resistance forces. Japan:
Vocal condemnation of L.E.T.F. actions and naval build-up in the Pacific.

2. Potential Future Threats:

Canada: Increasing alignment with anti-L.E.T.F. coalitions. France: Diplomatic support for
resistance forces in occupied territories.

### Outcome

1. Global Hit List Compiled:

The L.E.T.F. finalizes a list of high-value targets, including nations and individual leaders, for
future action.

2. Tensions Escalate:

The espionage operation deepens mistrust and accelerates anti-L.E.T.F. efforts globally.

3. Global Reactions

### United States

### Collaborative Stance

The U.S. continues to assist the L.E.T.F., justifying the espionage operations as part of a broader
effort to stabilize global power dynamics.

### United Nations

### Condemnation

The U.N. passes another resolution condemning martial law in South America and the invasion of
Australia, though enforcement remains limited.

### Resistance Movements

### South America

Guerrilla forces regroup in the Amazon, adapting to martial law measures.

### Australia

Insurgencies gain support from Japan, the U.K., and NATO allies, complicating L.E.T.F. control.

4. Summary

The L.E.T.F. declares martial law across South America, weakening resistance but
fueling civilian unrest.
Espionage operations using the SR-91 Poltergeist identify nations opposing the
L.E.T.F., resulting in a classified hit list of high-value targets.
Global tensions escalate as the L.E.T.F. tightens its grip on annexed territories while
preparing for potential conflicts with opposing nations.
