## Detailed Canon Account

Cuerbo Expands Anarchist Campaign

1. The L.E.T.F.'s Invasion of India

Operation Mystic Fade The Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (L.E.T.F.) begins a massive invasion campaign
targeting India, aiming to annex the subcontinent and expand Morganism into South Asia.

### Strategic Objectives

1. Territorial Expansion:

Subjugate India and integrate it as a key province within the L.E.T.F. empire.

2. Resource Access:

Gain control of India's vast population, agricultural output, and industrial capacity.

3. Religious Transformation:

Replace traditional Indian religions with Morganism, solidifying ideological control.

### Military Strategy

### Forces Deployed

### Ground Troops

3.5 million soldiers, including mechanized infantry, artillery, and elite urban combat units. Fade
Tanks and BMP-1s for frontline engagements.

### Air Force

MiG-21s, Su-17 bombers, and ceremonial propaganda jets broadcasting Morganist hymns.

### Naval Forces

Carrier strike groups blockade Indian ports like Mumbai and Chennai. Amphibious assault units target
coastal regions for rapid landings.

### Invasion Strategy

1. Northern Push:

Forces advance through Himalayan passes, targeting Delhi and key northern cities. Airstrikes
neutralize Indian military installations along the border.

2. Southern Landings:

Amphibious units secure coastal cities, including Mumbai and Chennai, cutting off supply routes.

3. Central Campaign:

Mechanized forces push into India's interior, aiming to capture industrial hubs and administrative
centers.

### Outcome

1. Partial Success:

Northern India falls quickly, with Delhi and other key cities under L.E.T.F. control. Southern and
central regions resist fiercely, with Indian forces rallying support.

2. Insurgencies Form:

Guerrilla resistance begins in rural and mountainous areas, supported by external funding.

2. Boombayah Funds Indian Forces

Under Madison's leadership, Boombayah covertly supports Indian forces, aiming to repel the L.E.T.F.
invasion and weaken its influence.

### Funding and Support

1. Financial Aid:

Boombayah channels billions of dollars to the Indian government for military supplies and logistics.

2. Arms Shipments:

Modernized tanks, aircraft, and naval vessels are delivered to Indian forces.

3. Strategic Advisors:

Boombayah sends military advisors to assist Indian commanders with strategy and coordination.

### Outcome

1. Indian Counteroffensive:

With Boombayah's support, Indian forces launch counterattacks in the south and east, slowing
L.E.T.F. advances.

2. Global Attention:

Boombayah's involvement sparks international support for India, further isolating the L.E.T.F.

3. Jaime Cuerbo's Anarchist Campaign in Boombayah

Unidentified Forces Arm Jaime Cuerbo The unidentified forces covertly arm Jaime Cuerbo, the
anarchist leader in Boombayah, escalating his campaign to destabilize Madison's government.

### Resources Provided

1. Aircraft:

A fleet of advanced fighters and bombers for air superiority and strikes on government
installations.

2. Naval Vessels:

Fast-attack ships and submarines to control coastal regions and disrupt supply lines.

3. Land Vehicles:

Modern tanks and armored vehicles to support ground offensives.

### Cuerbo's Campaign Strategy

1. Urban Insurgency:

Anarchist forces stage coordinated attacks on government buildings in major cities like So Paulo and
Rio de Janeiro.

2. Rural Mobilization:

Armed militias gain control over remote provinces, disrupting agriculture and logistics.

3. Propaganda Offensive:

Broadcasts criticize Madison's government and promote anarchist ideals, rallying public support.

### Outcome

1. Government Struggles:

Madison's administration faces mounting pressure as anarchists seize key regions.

2. Cuerbo Gains Momentum:

With advanced weaponry and external funding, Jaime Cuerbo's forces pose a serious threat to
Boombayah's stability.

4. Global Reactions

### Boombayah

### Focus on India

Madison prioritizes funding Indian forces but struggles to maintain control over Boombayah's own
territory.

### Internal Chaos

Jaime Cuerbo's campaign divides the nation, threatening to overthrow Madison's government.

### United Nations

### Calls for Peace

The U.N. condemns the L.E.T.F. invasion of India and calls for a ceasefire, but diplomatic efforts
fail to stop the conflict.

### India

### Resilience

Boombayah's support boosts morale and strengthens India's resistance against the L.E.T.F. invasion.

### Global Alliances

India garners additional support from nations opposing the L.E.T.F.

### L.E.T.F.

### Military Strain

The prolonged campaign in India stretches L.E.T.F. resources, with growing insurgencies in annexed
territories.

### Global Isolation

The invasion sparks widespread condemnation, further isolating the L.E.T.F. on the world stage.

5. Summary

The L.E.T.F. partially annexes northern India, but resistance supported by Boombayah
slows further advances.
Boombayah funds Indian forces, strengthening their counteroffensive and gaining
global support.
Jaime Cuerbo, armed by unidentified forces, escalates his anarchist campaign in
Boombayah, threatening to topple Madison's government.
Global tensions rise as the L.E.T.F. faces mounting resistance and internal instability
spreads in Boombayah.
