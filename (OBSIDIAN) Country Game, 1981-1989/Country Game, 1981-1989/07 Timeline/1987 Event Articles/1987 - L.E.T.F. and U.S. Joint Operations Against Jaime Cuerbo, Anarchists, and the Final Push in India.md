## Detailed Canon Account

the Final Push in India

1. L.E.T.F. and U.S. Operation to Assassinate Jaime Cuerbo

Operation Silent Fade Hearing of Jaime Cuerbo's revolt against both the U.S. and L.E.T.F., the two
powers launch a covert assassination operation to eliminate the anarchist leader and disrupt his
forces.

### Strategic Objectives

1. Neutralize Leadership:

Assassinate Jaime Cuerbo to dismantle the anarchist movement in Boombayah.

2. Reinforce Control:

Reassert U.S.-L.E.T.F. dominance in Boombayah's internal conflicts.

### Operation Details

### Forces Deployed

### L.E.T.F. Agents

Elite Morganist operatives trained in urban espionage and close combat.

### U.S. Special Forces

Navy SEALs and Delta Force units equipped with advanced stealth and intelligence tools.

### Plan

1. Phase 1: Intelligence Gathering:

U.S. reconnaissance drones and undercover operatives track Cuerbo's location. Informants reveal
Cuerbo's base of operations in Rio de Janeiro.

2. Phase 2: Infiltration:

L.E.T.F. agents infiltrate the compound, disabling external defenses and gathering real-time
intelligence.

3. Phase 3: Direct Strike:

U.S. special forces launch a precision raid, eliminating Jaime Cuerbo and key anarchist leaders.

### Outcome

1. Jaime Cuerbo Neutralized:

The operation successfully eliminates Jaime Cuerbo and dismantles his command structure.

2. Anarchist Forces Disarrayed:

The anarchist movement in Boombayah suffers significant setbacks, losing coordination and morale.

2. U.S. Operation Against Boombayah's Anarchist Forces

Operation Iron Resolve Following the assassination of Jaime Cuerbo, the United States launches a
full-scale military operation to eradicate anarchist forces in Boombayah, stabilizing the region for
L.E.T.F. and U.S. interests.

### Objectives

1. Destroy Anarchist Forces:

Eliminate remaining pockets of anarchist resistance.

2. Support L.E.T.F. Control:

Clear the way for L.E.T.F. annexation of Boombayah.

### Forces Deployed

### Ground Troops

300,000 soldiers, including mechanized infantry and artillery. Armored divisions with M1 Abrams
tanks and Bradley Fighting Vehicles.

### Air Support

F-15s, A-10 Warthogs, and Apache helicopters conduct close air support missions.

### Special Operations

Navy SEALs and Marine Raiders deploy to clear urban strongholds.

### Operation Strategy

1. Phase 1: Urban Combat:

U.S. forces launch coordinated offensives in major cities, including So Paulo and Braslia, targeting
anarchist bases.

2. Phase 2: Rural Suppression:

Mechanized units push into remote provinces to dismantle guerrilla camps.

3. Phase 3: Infrastructure Restoration:

Engineers repair critical infrastructure damaged in prior conflicts, stabilizing regions under
U.S.-L.E.T.F. control.

### Outcome

1. Anarchist Forces Crushed:

Major anarchist strongholds are destroyed, and their influence is significantly reduced.

2. Stability Restored:

The U.S. establishes control over key regions, preparing to hand them over to L.E.T.F. governance.

3. L.E.T.F. Final Push into India

Operation Eternal Jade Complete The L.E.T.F., having faced prolonged resistance in India, commits
all remaining resources to a final offensive to fully annex the country.

### Forces Deployed

### Ground Troops

5 million soldiers, including mechanized infantry, mountain warfare units, and urban combat
specialists.

### Air Force

MiG-21s and Su-17 bombers conduct continuous airstrikes. Propaganda planes broadcast Morganist
chants to weaken resistance morale.

### Final Offensive Strategy

### Northern Sweep

Target: Remaining resistance in northern India, including guerrilla forces in the Himalayas.
Outcome: Resistance is neutralized, securing northern borders.

### Central Dominance

Target: Industrial hubs and agricultural centers in the interior. Outcome: Central India falls under
full L.E.T.F. control.

### Southern Conquest

Target: Coastal cities like Chennai and Bengaluru. Outcome: Amphibious and mechanized forces secure
the south, completing the annexation.

### Outcome

1. India Fully Annexed:

India is declared the "Grand Southern Jade Province of the Fade."

2. Insurgencies Persist:

Small-scale resistance continues in rural areas, requiring ongoing military presence.

4. Global Reactions

### United States

### Victory in Boombayah

The successful dismantling of anarchist forces strengthens U.S. influence in South America, though
it sparks protests internationally.

### L.E.T.F.

### India Annexed

The annexation of India solidifies the L.E.T.F. as a dominant global power, though the cost strains
resources and morale.

### Boombayah

### Weakened State

Madison's government struggles to recover from the U.S. and L.E.T.F. interventions, losing its
foothold in South American politics.

### United Nations

### Condemnation

The U.N. issues resolutions against the U.S. and L.E.T.F., but geopolitical divisions prevent
unified action.

5. Summary

Jaime Cuerbo is assassinated, and the anarchist movement in Boombayah is
dismantled by U.S. and L.E.T.F. forces.
The United States crushes anarchist resistance in Boombayah, stabilizing the region
for L.E.T.F. control.
The L.E.T.F. completes its annexation of India, marking a major victory but facing
ongoing resistance.
Global tensions rise as the U.S. and L.E.T.F. assert their dominance in South America
and Asia.
