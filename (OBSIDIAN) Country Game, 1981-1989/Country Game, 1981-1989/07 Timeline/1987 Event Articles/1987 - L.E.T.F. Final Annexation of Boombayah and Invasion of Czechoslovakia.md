## Detailed Canon Account

1. L.E.T.F. Final Annexation of Boombayah

Operation Eternal Fade The Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (L.E.T.F.) commits its remaining resources to
fully annex Boombayah, aiming to crush all remaining resistance and solidify its control over South
America.

### Strategic Objectives

1. Total Annexation:

Bring all Boombayah territories under direct L.E.T.F. control.

2. Eradicate Resistance:

Neutralize guerrilla movements and dismantle Madison's government-in-exile.

### Military Strategy

### Forces Deployed

### Ground Troops

4 million soldiers, including mechanized infantry and urban combat units. Reinforcements from
Bolivia and Santo focus on remaining resistance strongholds.

### Air Force

Su-17 bombers and drones conduct precision strikes on guerrilla hideouts in the Amazon and urban
centers.

### Naval Forces

Amphibious units secure control over coastal cities and ports, ensuring no external supplies reach
resistance forces.

### Operation Plan

1. Phase 1: Central Sweep

Target: Remote provinces and Amazonian strongholds. Plan: Special forces and mechanized units
dismantle guerrilla networks. Outcome: Most rural resistance is eliminated, with remnants fleeing
deeper into the jungle.

2. Phase 2: Urban Control

Target: Remaining resistance in So Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, and Braslia. Plan: Urban combat
specialists engage in street battles to clear remaining resistance pockets. Outcome: So Paulo and
Rio de Janeiro are fully secured after weeks of intense fighting.

3. Phase 3: Final Neutralization

Target: Madison's forces in the Amazon Basin. Plan: Coordinated airstrikes and ground operations aim
to eliminate Madison's government-in-exile. Outcome: Madison's leadership is neutralized, ending
organized resistance.

### Outcome

1. Boombayah Annexed:

Boombayah is officially renamed the "Southern Grand Province of the Fade."

2. End of Resistance:

Organized resistance is eliminated, though isolated insurgent groups persist.

2. L.E.T.F. Invasion of Czechoslovakia

Operation Iron Curtain Fade From its base in the Eastern European Province of the Fade (Ukraine),
the L.E.T.F. launches a surprise invasion of Czechoslovakia, aiming to expand its influence further
into Europe.

### Strategic Objectives

1. Territorial Expansion:

Annex Czechoslovakia and integrate it into the L.E.T.F. empire.

2. European Domination:

Establish control over Central Europe, positioning for further expansions into NATO territories.

### Military Strategy

### Forces Deployed

### Ground Troops

1.5 million soldiers, including mechanized infantry and mountain warfare units. Fade Tanks and
artillery divisions spearhead the offensive.

### Air Force

MiG-21s and Su-17 bombers neutralize air defenses and strike key infrastructure.

### Special Forces

Operatives infiltrate Prague and Bratislava to disrupt local governments and communications.

### Invasion Plan

1. Phase 1: Eastern Advance

Target: Eastern Slovakia. Plan: Mechanized units advance from Ukraine, capturing major cities like
Koice. Outcome: Eastern Slovakia falls within days.

2. Phase 2: Central Push

Target: Bratislava and the Slovak heartland. Plan: Ground forces push westward, supported by
airstrikes on key infrastructure. Outcome: Bratislava is captured after moderate resistance.

3. Phase 3: Western Offensive

Target: Prague and western Czechoslovakia. Plan: Special forces disrupt defenses, allowing
mechanized units to secure Prague. Outcome: Prague falls, signaling the collapse of Czechoslovak
resistance.

### Outcome

1. Czechoslovakia Annexed:

Czechoslovakia is declared the "Central European Province of the Fade."

2. International Tensions Escalate:

NATO considers intervention, viewing the invasion as a direct threat to Europe.

3. Global Reactions

### United States

### Support for L.E.T.F.

The U.S. provides logistical and intelligence support but avoids direct involvement in Europe.

### United Nations

### Condemnation

The U.N. issues a resolution condemning the invasions of Boombayah and Czechoslovakia, calling for
immediate withdrawal.

### NATO

### Mobilization

NATO places forces on high alert, preparing for potential conflict with the L.E.T.F. in Europe.

### Resistance Movements

### Boombayah

Isolated insurgencies continue in the Amazon and southern regions, though their impact diminishes.

### Czechoslovakia

Nationalist groups form in the mountains, supported covertly by NATO.

4. Summary

The L.E.T.F. completes the annexation of Boombayah, renaming it the "Southern
Grand Province of the Fade."
The L.E.T.F. invades and annexes Czechoslovakia, extending its reach into Central
Europe and raising tensions with NATO.
Global backlash intensifies as the L.E.T.F. solidifies its control over South America and
Central Europe, sparking fears of a broader conflict.
