## Canon Context

Soviet mechanized divisions advance into East Germany but face fierce resistance from East German
Volksarmee (People's Army) units, who are reluctant to surrender their homeland. Poor coordination
among Soviet generals leads to logistical chaos, with supply lines failing within days. West German
and NATO reinforcements arrive quickly, bolstered by American air support, including A-10
Thunderbolts and F-15s. The Soviet advance is halted just outside Leipzig.
