## Canon Context

The Soviet Union, frustrated by East Germany's growing allegiance to NATO after recent events,
devises a plan to assassinate Erich Honecker, the General Secretary of East Germany's Socialist
Unity Party and de facto leader of the German Democratic Republic (GDR).
