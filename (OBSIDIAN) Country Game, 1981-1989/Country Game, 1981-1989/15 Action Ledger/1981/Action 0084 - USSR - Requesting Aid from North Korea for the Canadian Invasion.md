USSR: Requesting Aid from North Korea for the Canadian Invasion

## Canon Context

The Soviet Union formally requests North Korea's support in their planned invasion of Canada,
focusing on the destruction of maple trees. The USSR offers economic aid and access to captured
Cuban resources in exchange for North Korean troops and logistical support.
