## Canon Context

Kim Il-sung, North Korea's leader, sees an opportunity to strengthen ties with the USSR but
hesitates due to the logistical nightmare of deploying forces across the Pacific. Ultimately, North
Korea agrees to provide 2,000 infantry soldiers, small arms, and logistical supplies (like food and
basic vehicles), though they demand significant Soviet funding for their involvement.
