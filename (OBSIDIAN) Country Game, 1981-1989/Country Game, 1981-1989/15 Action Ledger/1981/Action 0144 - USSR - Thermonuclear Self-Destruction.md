## Canon Context

The USSR, under Premier Leonid Brezhnev's increasingly erratic leadership, launches a plan to
obliterate itself using its entire thermonuclear arsenal. The bizarre twist? The USSR also targets
Canada-perhaps as a final gesture of disdain for their maple trees and symbolic defiance against
NATO.
