**Operation Silent Fade** is the most significant intelligence operation of the 1961–1964 country game — a classified joint mission by [[Austria]], [[Sweden]], and the [[United States of America]] to simultaneously eliminate or capture all major leadership figures of the [[Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (LETF)]]. The operation's success triggers the collapse of the LETF's command structure and marks the beginning of the empire's terminal decline.

## Background

By 1963, the three non-LETF members of the [[Golden Lemons Alliance]] have grown increasingly alarmed by the LETF's escalating atrocities — including genocidal campaigns in China ([[Operation Jade Suppression]]), multi-nation nuclear strikes ([[Multi-Target Nuclear Campaign]]), mass depopulation operations in South America ([[Operation Silence of the South]]), and the nuclear annexation of Switzerland ([[Operation Alpine Fade]]).

Operating at the highest level of classification, Austria, Sweden, and the United States plan and execute a simultaneous strike against every major LETF leadership figure.

## Task Force Composition

**Codename**: Silent Fade

**Participating forces**:
- [[Austria]]: **Jagdkommando** (elite Alpine special forces) — infiltration and extraction specialists
- [[Sweden]]: **Särskilda Operationsgruppen (SOG)** — intelligence support, stealth operations, extraction
- [[United States of America]]: **Delta Force** and **DEVGRU** — direct action teams

**Total operatives**: 50 personnel (combined)

**Special equipment**:
- Stealth suits and encrypted communications gear
- [[E-12 Sentinel]] aircraft for surveillance and precision targeting
- Swedish [[S-61 Arctic Phantom]] stealth bombers for air support
- Falsified identities and LETF uniforms for infiltration

## Misdirection

To conceal the GLA's involvement, the task force plants evidence implicating [[China]] and North Korea in the assassinations. LETF loyalists are convinced that these neutral or rival states were responsible, diverting retaliation away from Austria, Sweden, and the United States.

## Aftermath

### LETF Leadership Collapse
The simultaneous elimination of all major LETF figures creates an immediate and catastrophic power vacuum. Regional governors and military commanders attempt to maintain control but face widespread mutiny and rebellion across occupied territories.

### Resistance Surge
Annexed territories worldwide see a dramatic rise in resistance activity, emboldened by the LETF's leaderless state. Chinese, Brazilian, Indian, South Korean, and other resistance movements intensify significantly.

### LETF Resurgence Attempt
The LETF launches [[Operation Eternal Fade]], assembling a new leadership roster from historical and fictional figures including [[King Darius III]], [[John Wilkes Booth (Fictional LETF Role)]], [[Shrek (LETF Symbolic Figure)]], [[George Washington (Fictional LETF Role)]], [[Kim Jong Un (Fictional LETF Role)]], and others. This resurgence effort fails to restore the LETF's former coherence or authority.

### GLA Solidarity
Austria, Sweden, and the United States celebrate the operation's success and solidify their reformed alliance, moving forward to execute [[Operation Iron Dawn]] against LETF-occupied Soviet territories.
