**Master Timeline** is an index article for the 1961-1964 country game vault. It organizes related fictional articles so the campaign can be read as a connected setting rather than as loose notes.

## Purpose
This page is used for navigation, continuity checking, and planning future turns. Index articles should remain concise but complete enough to show the major reading paths through the vault.

## February 1, 1961
- Game begins with [[United States of America]], [[Sweden]], [[Austria]], and [[Egypt]].
- [[Egypt]] reforms into [[Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (LETF)]].
- [[Morganism]] and LETF leadership are defined.

## 1961: Golden Lemons Expansion
- [[Golden Lemons Alliance]] forms.
- [[Operation Golden Dawn]] annexes [[Japan]] into [[North Japan]] and [[South Japan]].
- [[Operation Sacred Fade]] targets [[Israel]].
- [[Operation Cedar Fade]], [[Operation Crescent Fade]], and [[Operation Fades Crossing]] expand LETF through Lebanon, Syria, and Jordan.
- [[Operation Northern Fury]] gives [[Sweden]] a foothold in [[France]].
- [[Operation Mesopotamian Fade]] and [[Operation Anatolian Fade]] push through Iraq and Turkey.
- [[Operation Crimson Fade]] and [[Operation Baltic Fade]] shake the [[USSR]] and [[Poland]].
- [[Operation Nordic Dawn]], [[Operation Democratic Flame]], [[Treaty of Paris]], and [[Operation Carpathian Fade]] redraw Corsica, Afghanistan, France, and Romania.

## 1962: Technology Race and Overreach
- [[Project Arctic Shadow]], [[S-61 Arctic Phantom]], [[Project Shadowhawk]], and [[Shadowhawk Stealth Bomber]] escalate the stealth race.
- [[Operation Soviet Eclipse]] conquers remaining Soviet/Kazakh territory.
- [[Operation Eternal Steppe]] annexes Mongolia.
- [[People's Republic of La Plata (PRLP)]] receives LETF support.
- [[Operation Jade Fade]] and [[Operation Jade Suppression]] define the China arc.
- [[Operation Nordic Eclipse]] targets East Germany.
- [[Project Nordic Memory]], [[Operation Citizen's Dividend]], [[Swedish Tax Exemption Policy]], and [[U.S. Sanctions on China]] show domestic/economic turns.
- [[Multi-Target Nuclear Campaign]] and [[Operation Total Fade]] mark LETF maximum overreach.

## 1963: Crackdown, Resistance, and Rollback
- [[Operation Silent Fade]] targets LETF leadership.
- [[Operation Eternal Fade]], [[Operation Shadow Purge]], and [[Operation Shadow Recall]] show LETF's unstable rebound.
- [[Operation Alpine Fade]] targets [[Switzerland]].
- [[Operation Silence of the South]] triggers massive South American backlash.
- LETF loses Brazil, Argentina/Boombayah regions, India, South Korea, Indonesia, West Germany, Hungary, Switzerland, Iran, Saudi Arabia, and Canada.
- [[Operation Iron Dawn]] restores the [[USSR]].

## 1964 Track
- [[Final Collapse Dispatch]] sets up the total fall scenario.
- Use [[Open Plot Threads]] and [[Campaign Status Board]] to continue.
