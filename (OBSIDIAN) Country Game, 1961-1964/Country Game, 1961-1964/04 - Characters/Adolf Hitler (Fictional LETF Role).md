In the 1961–1964 country game, **Adolf Hitler** is assigned the fictional alternate-history role of **Vice Ruler of the Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (LETF)**. This is a surreal fictional casting used within the game's narrative — the LETF mythology deliberately incorporates historically infamous figures as part of its absurdist authoritarian aesthetic. The role does not reflect the game's players' views and should be understood strictly as an antagonist figure within a fictional setting.

## Role in the LETF

Within the LETF, Adolf Hitler serves as **Vice Ruler** and the right hand of [[Magnus Maximus]]. While Magnus Maximus controls military strategy, Hitler manages:
- **Political and governmental operations** — overseeing bureaucratic functions and territory administration.
- **Ideological enforcement** — ensuring [[Morganism]] doctrines are applied consistently across annexed territories.
- **Logistical coordination** — managing supply lines and ensuring operational continuity during campaigns.
- **Political administration** of the growing LETF empire.

The LETF mythology explicitly frames Hitler's role as that of a *loyal servant of the Fade's divine order*, rewriting his historical narrative through the lens of Morganist propaganda.

## Position in the Order of Morganism

In the [[Order of Morganism]], Hitler holds the position of **Vice-Leader**, second only to [[Magnus Maximus]]. Together with [[Alexander the Great (Fictional LETF Role)]], they form a leadership triumvirate that the campaign describes as *"feared globally for their strategic brilliance and unyielding ambition."*

## Campaign Activities

Hitler manages political and logistical efforts during virtually all major LETF campaigns, ensuring that the war machine runs while Magnus Maximus focuses on strategic command. He is credited with:
- Maintaining supply lines during the Japan campaign ([[Operation Golden Dawn]])
- Overseeing administrative transitions in annexed Middle Eastern territories
- Coordinating governance after major annexations including Israel, Lebanon, Syria, Jordan, Iraq, and Turkey

## Death and Aftermath

During [[Operation Silent Fade]] (1963), Adolf Hitler is tracked to a **fortified compound in Cairo**. Ground forces from the joint Austria-Sweden-USA task force breach the compound while Swedish stealth bombers provide aerial support. Hitler is **killed in the resulting airstrike**.

Following his death, the LETF initiates **[[Project Eternal Chancellor]]** — a genetic and biological research program to clone and revive him. Early trials produce viable clones, though none reach full functionality.
