**Gorg Morgan** is the **Patron Goddess of Purity and Virtue** in the [[Morganism|Morganist]] pantheon — the divine protector of the LETF's moral and cultural standards. She is the ideological enforcer of [[King Darius Morgan]]'s laws, ensuring that the followers of Morganism adhere to the code of conduct he has established.

## Divine Role and Mythology

Gorg Morgan's function within Morganist theology is to protect and enforce the *values of Morganism* — purity, virtue, and conformity to [[King Darius Morgan]]'s laws. She is understood as the divine guardian of cultural and behavioral orthodoxy, and her role distinguishes her from [[Mr. Clean]] (whose domain is punishment for grooming failures) and [[Vexbolts]] (whose domain is chaos itself).

Gorg Morgan's statues and shrines are **positioned near temples and sacred sites** throughout LETF-controlled territory — the most honored positions in Morganist public space. This placement signals that she guards the religion's sacred spaces themselves, rather than merely policing individual behavior.

## Relationship to King Darius Morgan

The name "Gorg Morgan" implies a familial or genealogical relationship to [[King Darius Morgan]], positioning her within the Morgan divine lineage alongside [[King Darius Morgan]] and the broader Morganist theological family. She reinforces the Morgan name's authority as the central divine family of the LETF state religion.

## Position in the Pantheon

Gorg Morgan sits on the [[Council of the Divine Fade]], representing the virtuous and pure dimension of Morganist ideology. She is essentially the complement to [[Vexbolts]]: where Vexbolts embodies chaos, Gorg Morgan embodies order; where Vexbolts is feared, Gorg Morgan is revered.

## Fate

Gorg Morgan is listed among the figures neutralized during [[Operation Silent Fade]] (1963), when coordinated simultaneous strikes by Austria, Sweden, and the USA eliminate LETF leadership figures across multiple locations. Advanced intelligence from [[E-12 Sentinel]] aircraft ensures precision targeting.
