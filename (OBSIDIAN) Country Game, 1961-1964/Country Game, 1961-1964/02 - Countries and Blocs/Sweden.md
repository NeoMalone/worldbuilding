**Sweden** is one of the four founding members of the [[Golden Lemons Alliance]], controlled by the game's mother figure. Sweden occupies a unique dual role throughout the campaign: it is simultaneously the alliance's primary humanitarian and scientific power, and a significant military expansionist in Europe. Sweden develops the first operational stealth bomber in the game timeline and plays a pivotal covert role in dismantling the [[Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (LETF)]] from 1963 onward.

## Special Ability

Sweden's defining national capability is possession of the **[[Cancer Vaccine]]** — a breakthrough medical technology that saves countless lives on the battlefield and establishes Sweden's global reputation as the alliance's humanitarian leader. The vaccine is administered by the [[Swedish Medical Corps]] across all GLA operational theaters.

## Role in the Golden Lemons Alliance

Sweden joins the [[Golden Lemons Alliance]] at formation in February 1961. Its initial role is as medical, reconnaissance, and logistical support — providing the [[Cancer Vaccine]], naval reconnaissance, and surgical air support rather than heavy ground forces. Over time, Sweden transitions into an independent military power with its own territorial ambitions.

## Military History

### Japan Campaign (1961)
Sweden provides medical support and reconnaissance during [[Operation Golden Dawn]], contributing fighter escort missions and naval reconnaissance. Swedish forces do not lead ground assaults but provide critical support to Austrian and LETF operations.

### France Campaign (1961)
Sweden declares war on [[France]], backed by overwhelming U.S. military support, in [[Operation Northern Fury]]. U.S. forces provide massive naval bombardments, amphibious landings, and air superiority; Swedish forces advance from Normandy toward Paris and from the Mediterranean coast.

**Territories taken**: Paris (flag raised over the Eiffel Tower), Marseille, Lyon, Strasbourg, and other eastern and southern French cities.

Following French defeat, Sweden signs the **[[Treaty of Paris]]** with France, retaining eastern France (Alsace, Lorraine, Burgundy, Provence) while France retains the west (Brittany, Normandy, Paris proper, Bordeaux).

### Corsica Campaign (1961)
Sweden launches [[Operation Nordic Dawn]], an amphibious invasion of [[Corsica]]. Swedish naval forces blockade the island while marines land near Ajaccio (the capital) and Bastia. Both cities fall with minimal resistance. Corsica is declared **Sweden's first major overseas possession** — a [[Corsica under Sweden|Swedish Mediterranean Territory]].

### East Germany Campaign (1962)
Sweden launches [[Operation Nordic Eclipse]], a full-scale annexation campaign against [[East Germany]]. Swedish air forces bomb Leipzig and Dresden, mechanized infantry advance on Berlin and Erfurt, and urban combat results in East Germany's complete annexation. East Germany is declared the **"Germanic Province of Sweden."**

## Technology Programs

### Project Arctic Shadow / S-61 Arctic Phantom (1961–1962)
Sweden accelerates its stealth bomber development program, [[Project Arctic Shadow]], after initial investment in 1961. With the program massively fast-tracked in late 1961, Sweden successfully designs, tests, and deploys the **[[S-61 Arctic Phantom]]** by early 1962 — making it the first operational stealth bomber in the game timeline.

**Capabilities**: Radar-evading angular design; 5,000-mile operational range; precision-guided munitions and tactical nuclear weapon delivery; limited operational deployment by March 1962.

The S-61 is first used in [[Operation Soviet Eclipse]] (1962) to conduct stealth raids on Soviet radar installations near Moscow and St. Petersburg.

### Project Nordic Memory (1962)
Sweden launches [[Project Nordic Memory]], a nationwide research initiative into curing Alzheimer's disease. 10% of GDP is invested; international research partnerships are formed; early-stage treatments show promise by 1963 with human trials projected for 1965. Sweden gains global acclaim and further cements its humanitarian reputation.

## Domestic Policies

- **[[Swedish Tax Exemption Policy]]** (1962): Sweden grants all citizens a full one-year exemption from income, sales, and corporate taxes. Consumer spending surges, the domestic economy booms, and government approval reaches record highs.
- **[[IKEA Expansion Program]]** (1962): Sweden constructs large numbers of IKEA stores throughout its controlled French territories, Corsica, and mainland Sweden (Stockholm, Gothenburg, Malmö). IKEA becomes a symbol of Swedish cultural and economic dominance.

## Covert Operations (1963)

Sweden contributes its elite **Särskilda Operationsgruppen (SOG)** special forces to [[Operation Silent Fade]], the classified joint operation eliminating all major LETF leaders. Swedish stealth bombers provide air support during the assault on [[Adolf Hitler (Fictional LETF Role)]]'s compound in Cairo; Swedish operatives personally extract [[King Darius Morgan]] from his temple in Alexandria.

Sweden also participates in [[Operation Iron Dawn]], providing naval blockades in the Black Sea, intelligence support via [[Golden Lemons Intelligence Network]] assets, and arms shipments to Soviet resistance fighters.

## Foreign Relations

- **[[Golden Lemons Alliance]]**: Core member; increasingly at odds with LETF from 1962; covertly hostile by 1963.
- **[[France]]**: Occupied and partially annexed; French resistance movements remain active in Swedish-held zones.
- **[[Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (LETF)]]**: Nominally allied; covertly hostile from 1962; actively working to eliminate LETF leadership by 1963.
- **[[Austria]]** and **[[United States of America]]**: Close partners; jointly execute anti-LETF operations.
