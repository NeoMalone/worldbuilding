**Morganism** is the official state religion and foundational ideology of the [[Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (LETF)]]. It fuses theocratic governance, an elaborate pantheon of divine figures, strict behavioral codes, and military imperialism into a single ideological system governing every dimension of life under LETF rule — from military doctrine to acceptable hairstyles. The religion is named after [[King Darius Morgan]], its living god and high priest.

## Origins

Morganism is established simultaneously with the formation of the LETF on February 1, 1961, when [[Egypt]] declares itself the Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade. Its founding myth centers on the sacred significance of the *Low Taper Fade* hairstyle as a mark of divine alignment and social discipline. The ideology draws from concepts of religious absolutism, cult of personality, and militaristic expansionism, blending them into a uniquely surreal theocratic framework.

## Core Tenets

1. **Obedience to the Divine Order** — Total loyalty to LETF leadership and the Morganist pantheon is mandatory. Disobedience is simultaneously a civil crime and a spiritual transgression.
2. **Sanctity of the Taper Fade** — The *Low Taper Fade* hairstyle is sacred. It signifies alignment with divine will. Citizens who fail to maintain an acceptable fade risk punishment from [[Mr. Clean]], goddess of happiness and lightning.
3. **Worship of Living and Celestial Gods** — The Morganist pantheon includes both living figures and mythologized deities (see below). Followers are required to pray to specific gods for specific purposes.
4. **Militarism as Devotion** — Military service and conquest are religious obligations. Every LETF campaign is framed as a divinely mandated mission to impose the Fade's order on a chaotic world.
5. **Cultural Unification Under the Fade** — All local cultures, religions, and identities in annexed territories must be replaced by Morganist principles. Diversity is treated as spiritual disorder.

## Rituals and Practices

- **Hair-Maintenance Ceremonies**: Regular communal events where taper fades are groomed and inspected for conformity to divine standards.
- **Military Parades as Religious Acts**: Attendance at LETF military parades is a religious duty, framing state power as divine spectacle.
- **Pre-Battle Prayer**: Soldiers pray to [[Skibidi Toilet]] (despite his trickster nature) and to [[Osama bin Laden (Fictional LETF Role)]] before air combat.
- **Blessing of Troops**: [[King Darius Morgan]] presides over religious blessings before major military campaigns.

## Political Function

Morganism is inseparable from governance. All LETF laws derive their authority from [[King Darius Morgan]]'s religious decrees. The [[Order of Morganism]] — the ruling political party — is explicitly a religious-political institution. Morganism is the mechanism through which the LETF maintains social control and legitimizes conquest.

In annexed territories, populations are required to immediately adopt Morganist practices. Temples, propaganda, statues of King Darius Morgan, and mandatory taper fade standards replace all local cultural markers.

## Symbols

- **Primary Emblem**: A taper fade silhouette surrounded by a halo of light.
- **Flag**: Black, gold, and white — representing power, purity, and eternal governance.
- **Slogan**: *"Through the Fade, we conquer; through Morganism, we unite."*

## Weaknesses and Decline

Morganism depends almost entirely on its living leadership figures for legitimacy. The assassination of [[King Darius Morgan]], [[Magnus Maximus]], and other key figures during [[Operation Silent Fade]] (1963) strips the religion of its symbolic authority. Without its living gods, Morganism rapidly loses its grip on annexed populations, accelerating resistance movements and defections across the LETF empire.
