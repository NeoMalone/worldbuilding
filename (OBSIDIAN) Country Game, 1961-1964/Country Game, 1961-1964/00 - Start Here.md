## Core Premise
The game begins on February 1, 1961 with four player countries: [[United States of America]], [[Sweden]], [[Austria]], and [[Egypt]], which reforms into the [[Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (LETF)]]. Their combined actions create the [[Golden Lemons Alliance]], a hyper-powerful bloc that initially cooperates, then fractures as LETF expansion becomes too extreme.

## Campaign Shape
1. 1961: alliance formation, Japan, the Middle East, France, Turkey, the first USSR crisis, and Eastern Europe.
2. 1962: stealth-bomber race, renewed USSR conquest, Mongolia, La Plata, China, East Germany, and the LETF overextension arc.
3. 1963: [[Operation Silent Fade]], LETF resurgence, resistance movements, Switzerland, South America, and [[Operation Iron Dawn]].
4. 1964 track: unresolved endgame material, centered on [[Final Collapse Dispatch]] and the global rollback of LETF power.
