The **IKEA Expansion Program** is [[Sweden]]'s 1962 cultural-economic expansion initiative — a coordinated construction of IKEA stores throughout Swedish-controlled territories that transforms the IKEA brand into a symbol of Swedish soft power and cultural-economic dominance across the Golden Lemons world. The program complements Sweden's military and humanitarian profile with a third dimension of national reach: commercial-cultural presence visible to occupied populations as a daily marker of Swedish administrative authority.

## Coverage

The Program constructs IKEA stores across Swedish territorial holdings:
- **Mainland Sweden**: Stockholm, Gothenburg, Malmö
- **[[Sweden-France Partition Zone]]**: Major French cities under Swedish administration (Marseille, Lyon, Strasbourg)
- **[[Corsica under Sweden]]**: Bastia and Ajaccio
- **Later expansion**: Following Operation Nordic Eclipse, IKEA presence extends into the Germanic Province of Sweden (East Germany)

## Strategic Function

The Program serves multiple strategic purposes:
- **Soft power projection**: IKEA stores provide visible, daily Swedish cultural presence in occupied territories
- **Economic integration**: Swedish commercial infrastructure ties occupied populations into Swedish supply chains and consumer patterns
- **Symbolic permanence**: The construction of permanent retail infrastructure signals Swedish intent to maintain long-term presence
- **Counter-narrative to humanitarian framing**: The IKEA presence makes Swedish administrative authority concretely visible, contrasting with abstract humanitarian framings provided by the [[Cancer Vaccine]] and [[Project Nordic Memory]]

## Domestic Effects

Within Sweden itself, the Expansion Program produces:
- Major construction activity supporting domestic employment
- Reinforcement of Swedish national pride in distinctive Swedish brands
- Coordination with the [[Swedish Tax Exemption Policy]] (also 1962) to drive consumer spending boom

## Reception

The Program's reception varies by territory:
- **Sweden**: Highly popular; reinforces national prestige
- **Partition Zone (France)**: Mixed reception; symbol of Swedish occupation but practical commercial benefit
- **Corsica**: Generally accepted; consistent with broader Swedish administrative integration
- **East Germany (post-Nordic Eclipse)**: Resented as occupation symbol; resistance networks target IKEA infrastructure as Swedish presence markers

## Cultural-Economic Synthesis

The IKEA Expansion Program represents Sweden's distinctive approach to occupation — combining military control with commercial-cultural integration rather than pure ideological imposition. This contrasts sharply with LETF Morganist administrative practice in adjacent territories. The combination of Swedish humanitarian framing (Cancer Vaccine, Nordic Memory) and commercial expansion (IKEA) gives Swedish-controlled territories a different occupation experience than LETF Fade Provinces.
