**Glossary** of recurring terms, acronyms, and concepts used throughout the 1961-1964 country game vault. Use this page to disambiguate jargon and find the canonical article for each term.

## Powers and Alliances

- **GLA** — [[Golden Lemons Alliance]]. The four-power coalition (Sweden, Austria, USA, LETF) formed February 1961.
- **LETF / L.E.T.F.** — [[Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (LETF)]]. Theocratic militarist empire reformed from Egypt on February 1, 1961.
- **PRLP** — [[People's Republic of La Plata (PRLP)]]. LETF-aligned client state, renamed from Argentina; receives 5 nuclear weapons.
- **NATO Remnants** — Surviving informal networks of former NATO personnel after the alliance's 1962 dissolution.
- **GLIN** — [[Golden Lemons Intelligence Network]]. Intelligence-sharing infrastructure across GLA powers.
- **United Nations Restoration Bloc** — 1963 coalition invoking UN legitimacy for anti-LETF operations.

## Doctrines and Ideologies

- **Morganism** — [[Morganism]]. State religion of the LETF; theocratic doctrine centered on King Darius Morgan and the divine pantheon.
- **Order of Morganism** — [[Order of Morganism]]. Ruling religious-political party of the LETF.
- **Council of the Divine Fade** — [[Council of the Divine Fade]]. Top doctrinal body governing Morganist religious authority.
- **Low Taper Fade** — The LETF national symbol; a hairstyle elevated to mandatory cultural-ideological signifier.
- **Fade** — Recurring LETF naming convention for operations and provinces; symbolically references the Low Taper Fade national identity.

## Province and Territory Terms

- **Province of the Fade** — Standard LETF designation for annexed territories. Examples: Negev Province of the Fade (Israel), Levantine Province (Lebanon), Syrian Province, Jordanian Province, Mesopotamian Province (Iraq), Turkish Province, Caspian Province, Western Province (Ukraine), Muscovite Province (Moscow region), Central Steppe Province (Kazakhstan), Mongolian Province, Carpathian Province (Romania), Baltic Province (Kaliningrad), Pacific Province (Hawaii), Sino Fade Provinces (China).
- **Total Fade Provinces** — The eleven provinces created during [[Operation Total Fade]]: Anglo (UK), Northern (Canada), Persian (Iran), Australasian (Australia), Amazonian (Brazil), Indian (India), Germanic (West Germany), Korean (South Korea), Archipelago (Indonesia), Arabian (Saudi Arabia), Danubian (Hungary).
- **Sweden-France Partition Zone** — Swedish-administered eastern France under the [[Treaty of Paris]].
- **Brest Province** — Austrian-administered former western Belarus.
- **Alpine Fade Province** — Annexed Switzerland after [[Operation Alpine Fade]].

## Operations Naming

- **Operation Fade** family — LETF operations: Sacred Fade, Cedar Fade, Crescent Fade, Fade's Crossing, Mesopotamian Fade, Anatolian Fade, Crimson Fade, Baltic Fade, Carpathian Fade, Eternal Steppe, Jade Fade, Jade Suppression, Total Fade, Eternal Fade, Shadow Purge, Shadow Recall, Alpine Fade, Silence of the South.
- **Operation Nordic** family — Swedish operations: Nordic Dawn (Corsica), Nordic Eclipse (East Germany).
- **Operation Silent** family — Anti-LETF GLA operations: Silent Halo (US support to Chinese resistance), Silent Fade (joint assassination of LETF leadership).
- **Operation Iron Dawn** — 1963 GLA campaign restoring the Soviet Union.
- **Multi-Target Nuclear Campaign** — LETF's 1962 simultaneous nuclear strike against 11 nations.

## Technology and Programs

- **Cancer Vaccine** — Sweden's signature humanitarian capability.
- **E-12 Sentinel** — US long-range reconnaissance aircraft (3 units gifted to Austria).
- **S-61 Arctic Phantom** — Swedish stealth bomber; first operational stealth bomber in the timeline.
- **Shadowhawk Stealth Bomber** — Austrian Mach 2.5 stealth bomber concept.
- **Project Eternal Chancellor** — LETF biological cloning initiative attempting to revive Hitler.

## Game Terms

- **Player Country** — One of the four original GLA powers controlled by the in-game players (USA, Sweden, Austria, Egypt/LETF).
- **Special Ability** — Each player country's defining national capability (e.g., Austria's bodybuilders, Sweden's cancer vaccine).
- **Conditional Surrender** — Negotiated partial defeat: cedes some territory while retaining sovereign autonomy elsewhere (used by Poland, the USSR's first surrender).
- **Sanctioned Article** — Articles tagged `sanitized` cover atrocity material at strategic level only, without procedural detail.
