**Content Boundaries for Future Writing** is a documentation article for maintaining the 1961-1964 country game vault.

## Overview
Use this vault like a fictional strategy-lore wiki, not an atrocity simulator.

## Keep
- geopolitical consequences,
- alliance shifts,
- resistance movements,
- internal regime instability,
- dramatic dispatches and scene tone,
- fictional propaganda as unreliable in-world text.

## Avoid
- graphic civilian harm,
- instructions for real weapons or illegal tactics,
- praise of extremist ideology,
- procedural assassination, terrorism, chemical, biological, or nuclear details.

## Good Replacement Style
Instead of describing atrocities directly, write about refugee flows, collapsing command networks, trials, propaganda fallout, sanctions, revolts, and the weakening of state legitimacy.

## Editorial role
Meta articles should keep the vault organized. They explain reading order, source coverage, safety boundaries, open plot hooks, and editing conventions.
