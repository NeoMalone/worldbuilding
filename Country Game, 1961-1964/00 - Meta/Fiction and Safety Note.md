**Fiction and Safety Note** is a documentation article for maintaining the 1961-1964 country game vault.

## Overview
This is an alternate-history fiction/game note. It is not a real-world claim, endorsement, or instruction.

Some source pages use real historical people, extremist figures, nuclear war, atrocities, and very dark war-crime material as fictional alt-history pieces. In this vault, those items are kept as story continuity, not praise, instruction, or approval.

## Handling Rule
When the source becomes graphic, the vault records only:
- the strategic story effect,
- the political fallout,
- the resistance response,
- and the way it changes the campaign map.

It does not preserve procedural details, graphic descriptions, or actionable harm.

## In-World Framing
LETF propaganda should be treated as unreliable. Notes about [[Morganism]], [[Order of Morganism]], and LETF leaders describe what the regime claims about itself, not what the vault endorses.

## Editorial role
Meta articles should keep the vault organized. They explain reading order, source coverage, safety boundaries, open plot hooks, and editing conventions.
