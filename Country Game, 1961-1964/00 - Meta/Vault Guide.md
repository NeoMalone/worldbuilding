## Folder Map
- `01 - Indexes`: maps of the vault.
- `02 - Countries and Blocs`: state actors, alliances, collapsed institutions, and restored governments.
- `03 - Factions and Ideologies`: parties, religions, intelligence networks, resistance movements.
- `04 - Characters`: named figures and symbolic leaders.
- `05 - Operations`: wars, campaigns, covert actions, and major crises.
- `06 - Territories and Provinces`: annexed or reorganized territories.
- `07 - Technology and Programs`: weapons, aircraft, medicine, economics, and cultural programs.
- `08 - Media and Scenes`: montage material, collapse dispatches, and tone references.
- `09 - Tables`: quick-reference lists for gameplay.
- `Templates`: reusable Obsidian note templates.

## How To Use This Vault
Use the indexes as launch pads. Every note links outward to the countries, operations, people, and provinces that matter. For a new turn, open [[Campaign Status Board]], pick a region, then update that country's note and any relevant operation note.

## Canon Levels
- `canon`: directly summarized from the PDF background.
- `framework`: organizational support added to make the Obsidian vault usable.
- `sanitized`: high-level handling of source material that became too graphic or extreme.
