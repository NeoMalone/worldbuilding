In the 1961–1964 country game, **George Washington** is assigned the fictional role of **LETF Labor Director** in the period following [[Operation Silent Fade]]. He is one of several historical and fictional figures recruited to fill the power vacuum left by the assassination of the original LETF leadership. Within the LETF's propaganda framework, he is presented as a legitimate "father figure" for the revived empire — ironically casting America's first president in service of an authoritarian occupying force.

## Context: The LETF Resurgence

Following [[Operation Silent Fade]] (1963), the LETF loses all major leaders — [[Magnus Maximus]], [[King Darius Morgan]], [[Adolf Hitler (Fictional LETF Role)]], and others. The LETF initiates [[Operation Eternal Fade]] to rebuild its leadership structure, assembling a diverse and eclectic roster of historical and fictional figures to fill the vacuum.

## Role in the LETF

George Washington is assigned to **spearhead the Labor Revival Initiative** in LETF-controlled territories. His role is framed through heavy propaganda as a return to *"traditional values"* and a *"New Era of Unity Through Work."*

Key actions under his direction:

1. **Mandatory Labor Policies**: All citizens in LETF territories are required to contribute to infrastructure projects, agriculture, or military support industries. Dissidents and resistance sympathizers are sent to labor camps.
2. **Labor Camps**: Overseen by LETF loyalists; political detainees are processed and assigned to military-industrial production.
3. **Propaganda Role**: Washington is presented in LETF media as the *"Architect of the Fade's Prosperity"* — a father figure rallying citizens around duty to the LETF's vision.
4. **Counter-resistance**: Washington's labor policies are paired with the LETF's [[Operation Shadow Recall]] to eliminate any resistance members who could undermine the program.

## Irony and Propaganda Function

The use of George Washington — historically associated with republican democracy and founding-era American ideals of freedom — as the face of LETF forced labor is deliberate propaganda irony. The LETF deploys his image specifically to undermine Western anti-LETF messaging, co-opting a symbol of liberty as a symbol of authoritarian productivity.

## Outcome

The Labor Revival Initiative produces short-term economic growth in LETF territories, increasing industrial and agricultural output. However, it simultaneously fuels widespread resentment and grows underground resistance movements, contributing to the LETF's eventual territorial losses.
