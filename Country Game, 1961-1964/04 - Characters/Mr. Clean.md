**Mr. Clean** is one of the primary divine figures of the [[Morganism|Morganist]] pantheon, holding the title of **Goddess of Happiness and Lightning**. Despite the masculine-coded name, Mr. Clean is characterized within Morganist theology as feminine-identified. She occupies a uniquely dual role: the deity of both joy and divine punishment.

## Divine Role and Mythology

Mr. Clean's theological function is to bring *joy and punishment in equal measure* to the subjects of the [[Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (LETF)]]. Her primary domain of enforcement is the sacred obligation to maintain a **low taper fade hairstyle** — those who fail to keep their fades properly groomed are said to invite her lightning-based wrath.

She is depicted in LETF iconography wearing **gleaming, immaculate armor** and projecting an electrifying presence. Her shrines appear throughout LETF military installations alongside those of [[Gorg Morgan]] and [[King Darius Morgan]], and her image is used in propaganda reminding citizens of the consequences of non-conformity.

Within the [[Morganism|Morganist]] ideological framework, Mr. Clean represents the positive-coercive dimension of LETF control: the promise of happiness for the compliant, and the certainty of violent retribution for the non-compliant. She functions as the divine justification for the LETF's enforcement of cultural and grooming standards.

## Position in the Pantheon

Mr. Clean is a member of the [[Council of the Divine Fade]], the governing religious body of the [[Order of Morganism]]. She represents the "happiness and enforcement" portfolio within the Council — responsible for maintaining morale among LETF forces while simultaneously deterring dissent through the threat of divine lightning.

## Campaign Significance

Mr. Clean's role is primarily symbolic and propagandistic rather than military. Her image is used throughout LETF-occupied territories to enforce behavioral compliance and frame obedience as spiritually rewarding. LETF propaganda films frequently invoke her as a goddess blessing those who maintain proper fade standards.

## Fate

Mr. Clean is listed as one of the figures neutralized during [[Operation Silent Fade]] (1963), when simultaneous strikes by the joint Austria-Sweden-USA task force eliminate all key LETF leadership figures. Her neutralization, alongside the deaths of [[King Darius Morgan]], [[Magnus Maximus]], and other divine figures, effectively dismantles the functional leadership of [[Morganism]].
