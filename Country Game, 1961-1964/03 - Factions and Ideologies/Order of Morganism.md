The **Order of Morganism** is the official ruling political party of the [[Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (LETF)]]. Chosen from a list of proposed names by LETF leadership, it embodies the fusion of religious doctrine, political authority, and global expansionism that defines the LETF state. The Order is simultaneously a governing party, a religious institution, and a military command structure.

## Naming

The Order of Morganism is selected from a shortlist that included *The Fade Party*, *Morganist Vanguard*, *The Unity of the Fade*, *Supreme Council of Fades*, *Fade Dominion Party*, *Fade Eternal Movement*, *Party of the Supreme Fade*, *Global Restoration Alliance*, and *Fade Ascendancy Party*. The name is selected for its emphasis on hierarchical religious order and its direct invocation of [[Morganism]].

## Core Tenets

1. **Worship of Divine Figures** — [[King Darius Morgan]], [[Magnus Maximus]], [[Adolf Hitler (Fictional LETF Role)]], [[Vexbolts]], [[Gorg Morgan]], [[Skibidi Toilet]], and [[Mr. Clean]] are revered as gods, saints, or cultural icons. All citizens must adhere to rituals and laws set by Morganism.
2. **Global Dominion** — All nations must be annexed into the LETF. Territorial expansion is framed as a divine mission: the Fade's order must be brought to a chaotic world.
3. **Cultural Supremacy** — All cultural and political systems in LETF-controlled territory must align with the Order's doctrine. Education, propaganda, and public policy enforce Morganist uniformity.
4. **Militaristic Expansion** — The LETF military is the enforcement arm of the Order, carrying out annexation campaigns as divine missions.
5. **Economic Control** — All territorial resources are directed toward the Order's military and religious priorities. Wealth is funneled to military development, religious institutions, and cultural dissemination.

## Symbols

- **Emblem**: A taper fade silhouette surrounded by a halo of light, representing divine leadership and unity.
- **Flag**: Black, gold, and white — power, purity, and eternal governance.

## Global Mission Statement

> *"The Order of Morganism is dedicated to creating a unified world under the principles of the Fade, blending religious devotion with political domination to forge a new global order."*

## Collapse

All principal Order leadership is eliminated during [[Operation Silent Fade]] (1963). A subsequent attempt to rebuild the Order with replacement figures — including [[King Darius III]], [[John Wilkes Booth (Fictional LETF Role)]], [[Shrek (LETF Symbolic Figure)]], [[George Washington (Fictional LETF Role)]], and [[Kim Jong Un (Fictional LETF Role)]] — fails to restore the organization's legitimacy or coherence in occupied territories. The parallel [[Project Eternal Chancellor]] initiative, aimed at cloning and reviving [[Adolf Hitler (Fictional LETF Role)]], produces early clones but no functional revival.
