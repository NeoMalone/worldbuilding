**Operation Eternal Fade** is the [[Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (LETF)]]'s emergency response to [[Operation Silent Fade]] — the 1963 covert operation by Austria, Sweden, and the United States that simultaneously eliminated all original LETF leadership. The operation is twofold: assembling a new leadership roster from available historical, fictional, and controversial figures; and launching the [[Operation Shadow Purge]] and [[Operation Shadow Recall]] counter-resistance campaigns to find those responsible.

## Background

[[Operation Silent Fade]] created a total leadership vacuum: [[Magnus Maximus]] (captured), [[Adolf Hitler (Fictional LETF Role)|Adolf Hitler]] (killed), [[Osama bin Laden (Fictional LETF Role)|Osama bin Laden]] (neutralized), [[King Darius Morgan]] (captured), [[Vexbolts]], [[Mr. Clean]], and [[Gorg Morgan]] (all neutralized) — every major LETF leadership figure eliminated within days. The LETF's regional governors attempt to maintain control but face widespread mutiny and rebellion across occupied territories.

Operation Eternal Fade is the LETF's answer: a rushed reconstruction of governance using whatever symbolic figures are available.

## Structural Nature of the New Leadership

The replacement roster's defining characteristic is its incoherence — historical figures ([[King Darius III]], [[John Wilkes Booth (Fictional LETF Role)|John Wilkes Booth]]), fictional figures ([[Shrek (LETF Symbolic Figure)|Shrek]]), and deliberately unsettling choices ([[Jeffrey Dahmer's Dad (Fictional LETF Role)|Jeffrey Dahmer's Dad]]). Rather than the original triumvirate's authority structure (Magnus Maximus / Hitler / Alexander the Great), the new leadership is a committee of surreal archetypes.

This reflects the LETF's post-Silent Fade state: an empire trying to project confidence through sheer symbolic force rather than the divine authority structure of Morganism's original pantheon. [[Shrek (LETF Symbolic Figure)]]'s appointment as Cultural Unification Symbol is the clearest single indicator of this shift — an empire so desperate that it puts an animated ogre in a leadership role.

## Counter-Resistance Actions

Operation Eternal Fade simultaneously authorizes two counter-resistance campaigns:
- **[[Operation Shadow Purge]]** — mass crackdown on resistance networks in occupied territories, led by John Wilkes Booth
- **[[Operation Shadow Recall]]** — covert mission to infiltrate U.S., Austrian, and Swedish facilities and assassinate captured LETF leaders (Magnus Maximus and King Darius Morgan are eliminated post-interrogation)

## Global Reactions

- **Golden Lemons (Austria, Sweden, U.S.)**: Covertly monitor the LETF's reconstruction efforts; increase funding and support for resistance movements; ramp up planning for [[Operation Iron Dawn]]
- **Resistance Movements**: Emboldened by the LETF's visibly unstable new leadership roster; guerrilla networks coordinate across occupied territories
- **Neutral nations**: View the eclectic new leadership as confirmation that the LETF is in terminal decline

## Legacy

Operation Eternal Fade succeeds in preventing the immediate collapse of the LETF empire but fails to restore its legitimacy. The new leadership cannot replicate the quasi-divine authority of the original Morganism pantheon. [[Operation Shadow Recall]]'s assassination of Magnus Maximus and King Darius Morgan eliminates the last leadership figures capable of providing continuity. The empire's momentum is broken; [[Operation Iron Dawn]] in late 1963 completes the rollback of former Soviet territories, and the LETF never recovers its earlier coherence.
