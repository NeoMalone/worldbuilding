The **Anglo Fade Province** is the LETF's administrative designation for the occupied [[United Kingdom]] following the 1962 [[Operation Total Fade]] annexation, preceded by a nuclear strike on London during the [[Multi-Target Nuclear Campaign]]. The province covers England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland under Morganist institutional governance. As the last major NATO anchor state, the UK's transformation into the Anglo Fade Province completes NATO's functional dissolution and removes the English-speaking world's primary independent military power outside the United States.

## Geography and Administration

LETF provincial administration is concentrated in surviving major cities — Manchester, Birmingham, Glasgow, Edinburgh — given London's nuclear devastation. The provincial capital function is distributed across multiple urban centers rather than concentrated in the destroyed governmental district. Morganist institutional frameworks replace British governmental, legal, and educational structures.

## Strategic Value

The province's strategic value is primarily symbolic and naval:
- **Atlantic positioning**: British coastal positions enable LETF Atlantic naval operations
- **Continental threat removal**: UK conquest eliminates a potential staging base for any anti-LETF coalition operating from outside Europe
- **Symbolic dominance**: The fall of the United Kingdom — historically a global imperial power — represents one of the campaign's most symbolically significant LETF achievements

## Resistance

British resistance leverages multiple geographic and institutional advantages:
- **Highlands and rural Wales**: Sanctuary zones that LETF garrison forces cannot effectively patrol
- **British military and intelligence institutions**: Strong organizational culture produces resistance networks with operational coherence
- **Commonwealth connections**: External communication channels with surviving Commonwealth networks
- **GLA support**: As the anti-LETF pivot proceeds, [[Golden Lemons Intelligence Network]] channels provide weapons and coordination

## Liberation

During the [[Final Collapse Dispatch]] period, Anglo Fade Province resistance expands as LETF garrison capacity deteriorates. UK liberation operations follow the broader collapse-phase pattern of resistance emerging from sanctuary zones into open territory as occupation administrative authority weakens.
