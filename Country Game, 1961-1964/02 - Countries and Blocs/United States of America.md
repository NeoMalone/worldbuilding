The **United States of America** is the military and financial backbone of the [[Golden Lemons Alliance]], controlled by the player. Possessing a classified defense budget of one quadrillion USD, the USA provides overwhelming air power, naval supremacy, and intelligence capabilities that underpin the vast majority of GLA military campaigns. The USA's relationship with the [[Lowest Egyptian Taper Fade (LETF)]] evolves from active ally to covert opponent over the course of the timeline.

## Special Ability

The USA's defining national capability is its **classified defense budget of 1 quadrillion USD** — a number so large it is kept secret from the world. This effectively makes the USA capable of funding and executing any conceivable military operation, sustaining multiple major wars simultaneously, and deploying the most advanced equipment available.

## Role in the Golden Lemons Alliance

The USA joins the [[Golden Lemons Alliance]] at formation in February 1961. Throughout the early and middle campaign, the USA acts as the alliance's enabler: providing the firepower, naval reach, and intelligence infrastructure that makes each GLA campaign viable. Unlike [[Austria]] and [[Sweden]], the USA rarely annexes territory directly, preferring to provide support to allied operations.

## Military Contributions

### Air Power
- **B-52 Stratofortresses**: Used in virtually every major campaign for strategic bombing of government buildings, military installations, and industrial centers.
- **F-105 Thunderchiefs**: Fighter-bomber escort missions, particularly in the Japan and Middle East campaigns.
- **F-100 Super Sabres**: Air escort for bombing raids during [[Operation Soviet Eclipse]].

### Naval Power
- **Carrier strike groups**: Deployed throughout the Pacific, Mediterranean, Persian Gulf, South China Sea, and Arctic Ocean.
- **Nuclear submarines**: Enforced naval blockades around Japan, Turkey, and the Black Sea coast.
- **Amphibious landing craft**: Used for contested coastal landings in Syria, France, Turkey, South Korea, Indonesia, Australia, and elsewhere.

### Intelligence
- **[[E-12 Sentinel]]**: Advanced long-range reconnaissance aircraft deployed to Japan for Pacific surveillance and gifted (3 units) to [[Austria]] for European intelligence gathering.
- **CIA operatives**: Active across Afghanistan, China, South America, and LETF-occupied territories.

## Key Actions

### Japan Campaign (1961)
The USA provides carrier-based air power, Marine landing forces, and nuclear submarines for the GLA's blockade and invasion of Japan ([[Operation Golden Dawn]]). U.S. forces establish the Pacific naval cordon that isolates Japan from external aid.

### Syria Campaign (1961)
The USA deploys aircraft carriers, B-52 bombers, and amphibious forces to the Syrian coast at Lattakia and Tartus in support of the LETF's [[Operation Crescent Fade]]. The USA provides overwhelming naval and air support without annexation intent.

### France Campaign (1961)
The USA provides *heavier and stronger support* for Sweden's war on France ([[Operation Northern Fury]]) than in any previous support role — including carrier groups off Normandy and the Mediterranean, B-52 strikes on Paris and Marseille, and amphibious Marine landings.

### Turkey Campaign (1961)
The USA expels [[Turkey]] from [[NATO]] and joins the LETF in [[Operation Anatolian Fade]], providing carrier blockades in the Aegean and Mediterranean, B-52 raids, and Marine amphibious landings at Izmir and along the southern Turkish coast.

### Afghanistan Coup (1961)
The USA launches [[Operation Democratic Flame]], a covert democratic coup against the Islamic Emirate of Afghanistan, which is sympathetic to the [[USSR]]. CIA operatives infiltrate Kabul, run propaganda campaigns, arm rebel factions, and ultimately topple the government, installing the **Democratic Republic of Afghanistan** in its place.

### Territory Transfers (1961)
The USA transfers two territories to the LETF:
- **Newport County, Rhode Island** → renamed the **"Rhode Fade Administrative Zone"** ([[LETF-US Disputed Territory (Newport County)]])
- **Hawaii (entire state)** → renamed the **[[Pacific Province of the Fade]]**, becoming a major LETF Pacific naval base (Pearl Harbor converted)

### USSR Campaigns (1961–1962)
The USA provides air and naval support for [[Operation Crimson Fade]] and [[Operation Soviet Eclipse]], including East Front operations using Pacific-based forces that land Marines near Vladivostok.

### China Operations (1962)
The USA sanctions China ([[U.S. Sanctions on China]]), deploying naval fleets to the South China Sea and Strait of Malacca. Simultaneously, the USA runs [[Operation Silent Halo]], covertly funding Chinese resistance forces defending hospitals while framing Iran as the weapons supplier.

### Chemical Weapons Program (1962)
The USA accelerates [[Project Shadow Mist]], its chemical weapons research program, testing nerve agents similar to VX and Sarin alongside delivery systems including artillery shells, missiles, and aerial dispersal units.

### Operation Total Fade (1962)
The USA provides military support to the LETF's [[Operation Total Fade]] annexation campaign across 11 nations, despite publicly condemning LETF's nuclear use.

### Operation Silent Fade (1963)
The USA contributes **DEVGRU** and **Delta Force** operatives to [[Operation Silent Fade]], the classified joint assassination of all LETF leaders. U.S. operatives personally neutralize [[Osama bin Laden (Fictional LETF Role)]] in his bunker in Riyadh. [[E-12 Sentinel]] aircraft provide precision targeting intelligence. Swedish stealth bombers carry out the airstrike that kills [[Adolf Hitler (Fictional LETF Role)]].

### Operation Iron Dawn (1963)
The USA deploys stealth bombers to disable LETF air defenses in Moscow, Volgograd, and Kiev; submarines disrupt LETF Arctic supply lines; and special forces lead strikes on LETF command centers. The operation successfully restores an independent Soviet Union.

## Internal Tensions

The USA's relationship with the LETF produces recurring internal contradictions:
- The USA publicly condemns LETF nuclear use while continuing to provide military support.
- The public begins questioning the transfer of Hawaii to the LETF.
- The USA's covert involvement in China through [[Operation Silent Halo]] strains LETF relations.
- By 1963, U.S. leadership commits fully to the anti-LETF effort.
